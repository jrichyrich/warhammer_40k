# Datasheet: Wolf Guard Headtakers

Wolf Guard Headtakers are elite, aggressive melee specialists who are often accompanied by fierce Hunting Wolves. They are experts in hunting down and eliminating high-value targets, using their speed and precision to bypass enemy defenses and strike at the heart of their command. They are a terrifying sight on the battlefield, a whirlwind of blades and teeth that leaves only death in its wake.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Let Loose the Wolves**
At the start of the battle, you can split this unit into two: one containing all **WOLF GUARD HEADTAKER** models and one containing all **HUNTING WOLF** models.

### **Headhunters**
At the start of the battle, select one enemy unit to be this unit's **Quarry**. Each time a model in this unit makes an attack that targets its **Quarry**, that attack has the **[DEVASTATING WOUNDS]** and **[PRECISION]** abilities.

### **Hunting Hounds**
While a **HUNTING WOLF** model in this unit is within 6" of one or more friendly **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** models, if it is not Battle-shocked, it has an Objective Control characteristic of 1.

## Wargear Abilities
### **Storm Shield**
The bearer has a 4+ invulnerable save.

## Unit Composition
*   1 Wolf Guard Headtaker Pack Leader
*   2-5 Wolf Guard Headtakers
*   0-5 Hunting Wolves
*   Every model is equipped with: heavy bolt pistol, master-crafted power weapon, storm shield.
