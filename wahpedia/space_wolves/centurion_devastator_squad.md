# Datasheet: Centurion Devastator Squad

Centurion Devastator Squads are heavily armored, walking fortresses designed to unleash a staggering amount of firepower while weathering the most intense enemy fire. Clad in massive exo-suits, they are equipped with a variety of heavy weapons that can melt armor, vaporize infantry, or suppress entire formations. Their role is one of direct and overwhelming application of force, clearing obstacles and ensuring the dominance of the Space Marine battle lines.
