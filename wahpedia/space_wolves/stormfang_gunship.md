# Datasheet: Stormfang Gunship

The Stormfang Gunship is a powerful aerial platform, equipped with devastating helfrost weaponry and designed to provide close air support for Space Wolves ground forces. Its primary weapon, the helfrost destructor, is capable of freezing enemy armor and monsters in their tracks, making them easy prey for the following assault.

## Abilities
### **Frozen Prey**
At the end of your Shooting phase, select one enemy **MONSTER** or **VEHICLE** unit that was hit by an attack made with this model’s **Helfrost destructor** this phase. Until the end of your opponent's next turn, subtract 2 from that unit's Move characteristic and subtract 2 from Advance and Charge rolls made for that unit.

### **Hover**
In your Command phase, this model can hover. If it does, until the start of your next Command phase, its Move characteristic becomes 20" and it loses the **AIRCRAFT** keyword and the associated rules for movement and attacks.

### **Transport**
This model has a transport capacity of 6 **ADEPTUS ASTARTES INFANTRY** models. Each **JUMP PACK**, **WULFEN**, **GRAVIS**, or **TERMINATOR** model takes up 2 spaces, and each **CENTURION** model takes up 3 spaces.
