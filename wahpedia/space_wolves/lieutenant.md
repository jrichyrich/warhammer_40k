# Datasheet: Lieutenant

Lieutenants are tactical specialists who assist their Captains in coordinating the efforts of their companies. They are experienced warriors, able to lead smaller detachments or support larger formations with their expertise. Their presence provides a significant boost to the offensive output of their units, and their tactical masters allows for hit-and-run tactics that can catch the enemy off guard.

### **Tactical Precision**
While this model is leading a unit, weapons equipped by models in that unit have the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability.

### **Target Priority**
This model's unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it **Fell Back**.
