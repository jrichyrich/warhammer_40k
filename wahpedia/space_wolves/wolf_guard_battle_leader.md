# Datasheet: Wolf Guard Battle Leader

Wolf Guard Battle Leaders are experienced warriors who serve as the right-hand men to their Wolf Lords. They are tactical masters, able to coordinate the efforts of their packs and lead them to victory in the most challenging of circumstances. They are also formidable warriors in their own right, often wielding powerful master-crafted weapons and specialized wargear.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Heroic Last Stand**
If this model is destroyed by a melee attack and has not fought this phase, roll one D6: on a 2+, it can fight before being removed from play.

### **Tempered Ferocity**
While this model is leading a unit, weapons equipped by models in that unit have the **[SUSTAINED HITS 1]** ability. Additionally, each time a model in that unit makes an attack targeting an enemy unit within 6" of this model, you can re-roll the Hit roll of 1.

## Wargear Abilities
### **Storm Shield**
The bearer has a 4+ invulnerable save and its Wounds characteristic is 6 instead of 4.
