# Datasheet: Wolf Guard Battle Leader

Wolf Guard Battle Leaders are experienced warriors who serve as the right-hand men to their Wolf Lords. They are tactical masters, able to coordinate the efforts of their packs and lead them to victory in the most challenging of circumstances. They are also formidable warriors in their own right, often wielding powerful master-crafted weapons and specialized wargear.
