# Datasheet: Blood Claws

Blood Claws are the youngest and most aggressive of the Space Wolves. They are eager to prove themselves in battle, often charging headlong into the fray with little regard for their own safety. While they lack the experience of their older brothers, their raw enthusiasm and fierce melee skills make them a potent force on the battlefield.
