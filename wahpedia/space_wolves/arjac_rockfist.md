# Datasheet: Arjac Rockfist

Arjac Rockfist, the Anvil of Fenris, is the personal champion of Logan Grimnar. He is a giant of a man, even for a Space Marine, and his strength is legendary. He is a master of defense, able to weather any storm and strike back with his massive hammer, Foehammer.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Anvil of Endurance**
While this model is leading a unit, each time a model in that unit is destroyed by a melee attack, if that model has not fought this phase, roll one D6: on a 4+, do not remove it from play. The destroyed model can fight after the attacking model’s unit has finished making its attacks, and is then removed from play.

### **Champion of the Kingsguard**
Each time this model makes a melee attack targeting a **CHARACTER** unit, you can re-roll the Hit roll and you can re-roll the Wound roll.
