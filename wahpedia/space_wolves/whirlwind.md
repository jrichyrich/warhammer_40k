# Datasheet: Whirlwind

The Whirlwind is a dedicated artillery tank that provides long-range indirect fire support for Space Marine armies. Built on the Rhino chassis and featuring a specialized missile launcher system, it can unleash a devastating barrage of high-explosive or incendiary missiles to suppress enemy infantry and disrupt their formations. Its ability to strike from behind cover and target hidden enemies makes it an invaluable asset for any mission requiring area denial and suppression.

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Whirlwind Suppression**
In your Shooting phase, after this model has shot, select one enemy **INFANTRY** unit hit by one or more attacks made by this model's Whirlwind missiles this phase. Until the end of your opponent's next turn, that enemy unit is suppressed. While a unit is suppressed, it cannot be selected to make an **Overwatch** attack and each time a model in that unit makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.

### **Damaged: 1-4 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-4 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
