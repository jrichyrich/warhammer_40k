# Datasheet: Long Fangs

Long Fangs are the oldest and most seasoned warriors of the Space Wolves, veterans of countless battles who have seen empires rise and fall. They are masters of heavy weaponry, able to unleash a devastating hail of fire with cold, calculating precision. Their experience allows them to identify and exploit the weaknesses of any foe, making them a cornerstone of the Chapter's ranged support.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Fire Discipline**
If this unit **Remains Stationary**, until the end of the turn, each time a model in this unit makes a ranged attack, you can re-roll the Hit roll of 1.

### **Armorium Cherub**
Once per battle, after a model in this unit makes a Hit roll for a ranged attack, you can change the result of that Hit roll to an unmodified 6.

## Unit Composition
*   1 Long Fang Pack Leader
*   4-5 Long Fangs
*   Every model is equipped with: bolt pistol, close combat weapon. The Pack Leader is also equipped with a boltgun. Each Long Fang is also equipped with one of the heavy weapons listed above.
