# Datasheet: Canis Wolfborn

Canis Wolfborn is a feral warrior, raised by wolves on the death world of Fenris. He is a master of the hunt, leading packs of Fenrisian Wolves and Thunderwolf Cavalry with a primal authority. In battle, he is a whirlwind of claws and teeth, striking with the speed and ferocity of the animals he calls kin.
