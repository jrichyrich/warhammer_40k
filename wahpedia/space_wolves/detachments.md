# Space Wolves Detachments

The Space Wolves have access to a wide array of detachments, ranging from their unique Chapter-specific sagas to the standard tactical formations of the Adeptus Astartes.

## Unique Space Wolves Detachments
*   [Champions of Fenris](detachment_champions_of_fenris.md) - Focuses on the heroic deeds (Sagas) of characters.
*   [Saga of the Hunter](detachment_saga_of_the_hunter.md) - Emphasizes speed, infiltration, and shock assaults.
*   [Saga of the Bold](detachment_saga_of_the_bold.md) - Centers on champions leading from the front with heroic boasts.
*   [Saga of the Beastslayer](detachment_saga_of_the_beastslayer.md) - Specializes in hunting down Monsters and Vehicles.
*   [Saga of the Great Wolf](detachment_saga_of_the_great_wolf.md) - Highlights the leadership and inspirational presence of the Wolf Lords.

## Standard Space Marine Detachments
Space Wolves can also use the following standard formations from *Codex: Space Marines*:
*   [Gladius Task Force](detachment_gladius_task_force.md) - Balanced and adaptable formation.
*   [Anvil Siege Force](detachment_anvil_siege_force.md) - Stationary defensive firepower.
*   [Ironstorm Spearhead](detachment_ironstorm_spearhead.md) - Armoured and vehicle-focused.
*   [Firestorm Assault Force](detachment_firestorm_assault_force.md) - Close-range and transport-based aggression.
*   [Stormlance Task Force](detachment_stormlance_task_force.md) - Highly mobile and cavalry-focused.
*   [Vanguard Spearhead](detachment_vanguard_spearhead.md) - Stealth, infiltration, and surgical strikes.
*   [1st Company Task Force](detachment_1st_company_task_force.md) - Elite veteran and terminator focus.
*   [Librarius Conclave](detachment_librarius_conclave.md) - Psychic-focused support.
*   [Bastion Task Force](detachment_bastion_task_force.md) - Battleline and tactical coordination.
*   [Orbital Assault Force](detachment_orbital_assault_force.md) - Deep strike and rapid deployment.
