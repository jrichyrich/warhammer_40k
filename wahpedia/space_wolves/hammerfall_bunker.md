# Datasheet: Hammerfall Bunker

The Hammerfall Bunker is a massive automated fortification deployed from orbit to secure vital territory and provide a nearly impenetrable defensive anchor. Armed with a variety of heavy weapon arrays, it can unleash a devastating hail of fire against any enemy foolish enough to approach. Its role is one of static and overwhelming defense, providing cover for friendly units and ensuring the dominance of the Space Marine battle lines in the most hostile environments.

## Abilities

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Ceramite Cover**
Each time a ranged attack is made against a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit, if this model is not fully visible to the attacker, that unit has the **Benefit of Cover**.

### **Defensive Array**
You can target this model with the **Fire Overwatch** Stratagem for 0CP, even if you have already used that Stratagem on another unit this turn.

### **Damaged: 1-5 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-5 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
