# Datasheet: Bjorn the Fell-handed

Bjorn the Fell-handed is the oldest living warrior in the Imperium, a venerable Dreadnought who has served the Space Wolves for over ten thousand years. He is a living legend, a repository of wisdom and a source of inspiration for all who fight alongside him.

### **Legendary Tenacity**
