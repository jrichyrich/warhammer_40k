# Datasheet: Assault Intercessor Squad

Assault Intercessor Squads are aggressive melee-focused units designed to clear enemies off objectives and dominate close-quarters combat. Armed with Astartes chainswords and heavy bolt pistols, they are adept at tearing through infantry and securing vital positions. Their hard-won experience and primal ferocity make them a terrifying force on the battlefield.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Shock Assault**
Each time a model in this unit makes a melee attack, re-roll a Wound roll of 1. If the target of that attack is within range of an objective marker, you can re-roll the Wound roll instead.

## Unit Composition
*   1 Assault Intercessor Sergeant
*   4-9 Assault Intercessors
*   Every model is equipped with: heavy bolt pistol, Astartes chainsword.
