# Detachment: Bastion Task Force

The Bastion Task Force is a formation that emphasizes the defensive and tactical capabilities of the Adeptus Astartes' Battleline units. It is characterized by its ability to hold positions and coordinate its attacks.

## Detachment Rule: Interlocking Tactics
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES BATTLELINE** units from your army are eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which they Advanced or Fell Back. In addition, each time a **BATTLELINE** unit from your army makes an attack, the target unit is "scanned" until the end of the phase; while an enemy unit is scanned, other **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units from your army can re-roll Hit rolls of 1 against it.

## Enhancements
*   **Eye of the Primarch (10 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer's unit has the [PRECISION] ability.
*   **Hero of the Chapter (20 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer gains the **BATTLELINE** keyword.
*   **Blades of Valour (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Add 1 to the AP characteristic of the bearer's unit's melee weapons.
*   **Bombast Omnivox (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Once per battle round, when you target the bearer's unit with a Stratagem, roll one D6: on a 4+, you gain 1CP.

## Stratagems
*   **Codex Discipline (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One unit can re-roll Hit rolls of 1 (and Wound rolls of 1 if targeting a scanned unit).
*   **Guided Disruption (1CP):** (Shooting phase) One enemy unit (excluding **MONSTERS** and **VEHICLES**) is suppressed (-2 from its Move and Charge rolls).
*   **Light of Vengeance (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One unit gains the [LETHAL HITS] or [SUSTAINED HITS 1] ability against a scanned unit.
*   **Shock Bombardment (1CP):** (Shooting phase) One scanned enemy unit subtracts 1 from its Hit rolls until the end of the next phase.
*   **Angels Defiant (1CP):** (Incoming attack) One unit subtracts 1 from the Wound roll if the attack's Strength is greater than the unit's Toughness.
*   **Heresy Undone (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One non-**BATTLELINE** unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced or Fell Back if it targets a scanned enemy.
