# Datasheet: Murderfang

Murderfang is a force of pure, animalistic destruction, a warrior whose mind has been lost to the Curse of the Wulfen but whose physical prowess remains unparalleled. He is a living weapon, a whirlwind of claws and fire that tears through anything in its path.

## Abilities
### **Murder-maker**
Each time an enemy unit targets this model, after that unit has finished making its attacks, this model can either shoot as though it were your Shooting phase or fight as though it were the Fight phase.

### **Bestial Fury**
You can re-roll Advance and Charge rolls made for this model.
