# Datasheet: Vindicator

The Vindicator is a heavy siege tank designed to breach the most formidable enemy fortifications and eliminate concentrated resistance. Built on the Rhino chassis and featuring a reinforced front-mounted siege shield, it is equipped with a massive demolisher cannon capable of unleashing a devastating blast of high-explosive shells. Its role is one of direct and brutal application of force, clearing obstacles and vaporizing anything in its path.

### **Siege Shield**
This model is eligible to shoot its **demolisher cannon** even while it is within Engagement Range of one or more enemy units. When doing so, it does not suffer the usual -1 penalty to Hit rolls for being within Engagement Range.

### **Damaged: 1-4 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-4 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
