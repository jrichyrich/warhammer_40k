# Datasheet: Wolf Priest

Wolf Priests are the spiritual and physical healers of the Space Wolves, combining the roles of Chaplain and Apothecary. They are responsible for the well-being of their brothers' souls and bodies, leading them in prayer and tending to their wounds in the heat of battle. They are also fierce warriors, inspiring their packs with their presence and their skill in combat.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Healing Balms**
While this model is leading a unit, in your Command phase, you can select one model in that unit that has been destroyed (excluding **CHARACTER** models) and return it to the battlefield with all its wounds remaining.

### **Litany of Hate**
While this model is leading a unit, each time a model in that unit makes a melee attack, add 1 to the Wound roll.
