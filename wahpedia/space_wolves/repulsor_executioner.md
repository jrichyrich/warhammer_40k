# Datasheet: Repulsor Executioner

The Repulsor Executioner is a heavy gunship variant of the Repulsor chassis, designed to deliver overwhelming firepower against the most formidable targets. While it sacrifices half of its transport capacity, it houses a massive primary turret weapon—either a macro plasma incinerator or a heavy laser destroyer—making it one of the deadliest tanks in the Space Marine arsenal. Its advanced targeting systems and heavy armor allow it to dominate the battlefield and eliminate any threat to the Imperium.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D6**
*   **Hover**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Executioner**
Each time this model makes an attack against a unit that is **Below Half-strength**, add 1 to the Hit roll.

### **Damaged: 1-5 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-5 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
