# Army Rules: Space Wolves

The Space Wolves, being a specialized Chapter of the Adeptus Astartes, have access to both the core **Oath of Moment** rule and their unique Chapter-specific mechanics.

## Oath of Moment
At the start of your Command phase, select one enemy unit. Until your next Command phase, each time a model with this ability makes an attack that targets that unit, you can re-roll the Hit roll.

## Curse of the Wulfen
Units within 6" of a **Space Wolves Character** (or 12" of a **Wolf Priest**) gain a bonus to their Objective Control (OC) characteristic (+1 for **Infantry**, +3 for **Vehicles**), provided they are not **Battle-shocked**.

## Sagas
Each Space Wolves detachment includes a specific "Saga" (a heroic feat). Once the conditions of the Saga are met during battle, additional powerful effects are unlocked for the remainder of the game.

## Sons of Russ (Restrictions)
A Space Wolves army cannot include the following units:
*   **Apothecaries**
*   **Devastator Squads**
*   **Tactical Squads**

It also cannot include units from any other Space Marine Chapter (e.g., **Ultramarines**, **Blood Angels**, etc.).
