# Datasheet: Dreadnought

The standard Dreadnought is a venerable combat walker, housing a fallen hero of the Chapter who continues to serve even after suffering mortal wounds. Equipped with a variety of heavy weapons and powerful melee limbs, it is a versatile and durable asset on any battlefield.
