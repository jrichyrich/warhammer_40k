# Detachment: Ironstorm Spearhead

The Ironstorm Spearhead is a formation that emphasizes the power and resilience of the Adeptus Astartes' armoured columns and heavy walkers. It is characterized by its ability to maintain its vehicles in the thick of battle.

## Detachment Rule: Armoured Wrath
Each time an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army is selected to shoot or fight, you can re-roll one Hit roll, one Wound roll, or one Damage roll.

## Enhancements
*   **Target Augury Web (30 pts):** **TECHMARINE** model only. Aura: While a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES VEHICLE** unit is within 6" of the bearer, ranged weapons equipped by models in that unit have the [LETHAL HITS] ability.
*   **The Flesh is Weak (10 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer has the Feel No Pain 4+ ability.
*   **Adept of the Omnissiah (35 pts):** **TECHMARINE** model only. Once per battle round, when an attack is allocated to a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES VEHICLE** unit within 6" of the bearer, you can change the Damage characteristic of that attack to 0.
*   **Master of Machine War (20 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Aura: While a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES VEHICLE** unit is within 6" of the bearer, that unit is eligible to shoot in a turn in which it Advanced or Fell Back.

## Stratagems
*   **Unbowed Conviction (1CP):** (Incoming attack) One unit ignores all modifiers to its characteristics and to rolls made for models in that unit.
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Mercy is Weakness (2CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One unit's attacks have the [SUSTAINED HITS 1] ability; if targeting a unit below its starting strength, they score Critical Hits on 5+.
*   **Vengeful Animus (1CP):** (Destroyed) One **VEHICLE** model automatically triggers its Deadly Demise ability.
*   **Ancient Fury (1CP):** (Command phase) One **WALKER** unit gains +1 to its Move, Toughness, Leadership, and Objective Control characteristics, and +1 to its Hit rolls.
*   **Power of the Machine Spirit (1CP):** (Enemy Shooting phase) After an enemy unit resolves its attacks and your **VEHICLE** is below half-strength, your vehicle can shoot as if it were your Shooting phase.
