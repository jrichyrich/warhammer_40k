# Detachment: Saga of the Hunter

This detachment focuses on lightning speed and shock assaults, utilizing outflanking and infiltration.

## Detachment Rule: Pack’s Quarry
At their Wolf Lord’s command, the Space Wolves advance upon their prey with lupine hunger, singling out the weak and overwhelming the strong with concentrated force and furious aggression.

*   **Effect:** Each time a model in a **SPACE WOLVES** unit from your army makes a melee attack that targets an enemy unit, if that enemy unit is within Engagement Range of one or more other **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units from your army, or if the attacking unit contains more models than that enemy unit:
    *   Add 1 to the Hit roll.
    *   If your **Saga is completed** (see below), add 1 to the Wound roll as well.

### **Saga of the Hunter (Completion Condition)**
*   At the start of the first battle round, your Quarry tally is 0.
*   Each time an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army fights, after all of those attacks have been resolved, add 1 to your Quarry tally for each enemy unit destroyed by those attacks.
*   Once your Quarry tally is equal to or greater than the number shown below (depending on the battle size), your Saga is completed:
    *   **Incursion:** 2
    *   **Strike Force:** 3
    *   **Onslaught:** 4

## Enhancements
*   **Swift Hunter (20 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Models in the bearer’s unit have the **Scouts 7"** ability.
*   **Fenrisian Grit (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer has the **Feel No Pain 4+** ability.
*   **Wolf Master (5 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. In your Command phase, select one friendly **SPACE WOLVES** unit within 9" of the bearer. Until the start of your next Command phase, teeth and claws and Tyrnak and Fenrir weapons equipped by models in that unit have the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability.
*   **Feral Rage (10 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of melee weapons equipped by the bearer. Each time the bearer ends a Charge move, until the end of the turn, add an additional 1 to the Attacks characteristic of those weapons.

## Stratagems
*   **Hunters’ Trail (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* Used in the **Fight phase**. Target one **SPACE WOLVES** unit (excluding Monsters/Vehicles). Until the end of the phase, Pile-in or Consolidation moves can be up to 6" instead of 3". The unit does not need to end closer to the closest enemy model, provided it ends as close as possible to the closest enemy unit.
*   **Territorial Advantage (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* Used in the **Fight phase**, just after an enemy unit is destroyed. Target the attacking unit. Select one objective marker you control within range; it remains under your control until the opponent’s Level of Control is greater than yours at the end of a phase.
*   **Overwhelming Onslaught (1CP):** *Battle Tactic.* Used in the **Fight phase**, just after an enemy unit has selected its targets. Target two **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units (or one **BEASTS** unit) within Engagement Range of that enemy. Until the end of the phase, subtract 1 from the enemy's Hit rolls.
*   **Chosen Prey (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* Used in your **Movement phase**, just after a **SPACE WOLVES** unit Falls Back. That unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in the same turn it Fell Back.
*   **Bounding Advance (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* Used in your **Movement or Charge phase**. Target one **SPACE WOLVES INFANTRY** or **BEASTS** unit. Until the end of the phase, models can move through other models (excluding Titanic). They can move within Engagement Range of enemies but cannot end a move there (unless charging).
*   **Marked for Destruction (1CP):** *Battle Tactic.* Used in your **Shooting phase**. Target two **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units (excluding Beasts). Select one enemy unit visible to both. Until the end of the phase, your units can only target that enemy, and you re-roll Wound rolls of 1 against it.
