# Detachment: Gladius Task Force

The Gladius Task Force is the most versatile formation available to a Space Marine commander, emphasizing the core tenets of the Codex Astartes and the ability to adapt to any battlefield situation through the use of Combat Doctrines.

## Detachment Rule: Combat Doctrines
At the start of your Command phase, you can select one of the following Combat Doctrines. Each Combat Doctrine can only be selected once per battle. The selected doctrine applies to all **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units from your army until the start of your next Command phase.

*   **Devastator Doctrine:** Units from your army are eligible to shoot in a turn in which they Advanced.
*   **Tactical Doctrine:** Units from your army are eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which they Fell Back.
*   **Assault Doctrine:** Units from your army are eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which they Advanced.

## Enhancements
*   **Artificer Armour (10 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer has a Save characteristic of 2+ and the Feel No Pain 5+ ability.
*   **The Honour Vehement (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Add 1 to the Attacks and Strength characteristics of the bearer's melee weapons. While the **Assault Doctrine** is active for your army, add 2 to those characteristics instead.
*   **Adept of the Codex (20 pts):** **CAPTAIN** model only. At the start of your Command phase, if the bearer is on the battlefield, you can instead select the **Tactical Doctrine** to be active for the bearer's unit only, even if you have already selected that doctrine this battle.
*   **Fire Discipline (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, ranged weapons equipped by models in that unit have the [SUSTAINED HITS 1] ability. While the **Devastator Doctrine** is active for your army, each time a model in that unit makes a ranged attack, a successful unmodified Hit roll of 5+ scores a Critical Hit.

## Stratagems
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Only in Death Does Duty End (2CP):** (Fight phase) A model in the unit that is destroyed can fight before being removed.
*   **Honour the Chapter (1CP):** (Fight phase) Melee weapons gain [LANCE]; while in **Assault Doctrine**, they also gain an extra -1 AP.
*   **Adaptive Strategy (1CP):** (Command phase) Select one unit; until the start of your next Command phase, that unit is treated as being in a Combat Doctrine of your choice.
*   **Storm of Fire (1CP):** (Shooting phase) Ranged weapons gain [IGNORES COVER]; while in **Devastator Doctrine**, they also gain an extra -1 AP.
*   **Squad Tactics (1CP):** (Enemy move) When an enemy unit ends a move within 9", your unit can move D6" (or 6" if in **Tactical Doctrine**).
