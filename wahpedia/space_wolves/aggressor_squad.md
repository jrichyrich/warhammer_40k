# Datasheet: Aggressor Squad

Aggressor Squads are chunky, Gravis-armored units designed for close-range fire support and brutal melee combat. Armed with either flamestorm gauntlets for clearing hordes or auto boltstorm gauntlets and fragstorm grenade launchers for overwhelming fire, they are a formidable presence on the battlefield. Their advanced armor and specialized training allow them to weather significant punishment while delivering punishing return fire or crushing enemies in close quarters.

## Abilities

### **Close-quarters Firepower**
Each time a model in this unit makes a ranged attack that targets the closest eligible enemy unit, improve the Armour Penetration characteristic of that attack by 1.

## Unit Composition
