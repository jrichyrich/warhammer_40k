# Detachment: Librarius Conclave

The Librarius Conclave is a formation that emphasizes the psychic power and mystical abilities of the Adeptus Astartes' Psykers. It is characterized by its ability to harness the warp to empower its allies and devastate its foes.

## Detachment Rule: Psychic Disciplines
At the start of each battle round, you can select one of the following Psychic Disciplines. The selected discipline applies to all **ADEPTUS ASTARTES PSYKERS** from your army until the end of the battle round.

*   **Biomancy:** Add 2 to the Move characteristic of the unit.
*   **Divination:** Re-roll Hit and Wound rolls of 1 for the unit.
*   **Pyromancy:** Add 1 to the AP characteristic of ranged attacks within 12".
*   **Telekinesis:** Subtract 1 from the Strength characteristic of incoming ranged attacks.
*   **Telepathy:** Ignore all modifiers to the unit's Hit rolls and WS/BS characteristics.

## Enhancements
*   **Prescience (25 pts):** **PSYKER** model only. When an enemy unit ends a move within 9" of the bearer's unit, that unit can move up to D6" (or 6" if **Divination** is active).
*   **Celerity (30 pts):** **PSYKER** model only. The bearer's unit is eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced (and Fell Back if **Biomancy** is active).
*   **Obfuscation (20 pts):** **PSYKER** model only. Enemy units cannot target the bearer's unit with Overwatch; the unit has the **LONE OPERATIVE** ability against units more than 18" away if **Telepathy** is active.
*   **Fusillade (35 pts):** **PSYKER** model only. Ranged weapons equipped by models in the bearer's unit gain the [ANTI-MONSTER 5+] and [ANTI-VEHICLE 5+] abilities.

## Stratagems
*   **Sensory Assault (1CP):** (Shooting phase) Subtract 2 from the Move and Charge rolls of one enemy unit; that unit also becomes Battle-shocked if **Telepathy** is active.
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Fiery Shield (1CP):** (Enemy Shooting phase) Subtract 1 from Hit rolls of incoming attacks; attacker suffers from [HAZARDOUS] if **Pyromancy** is active.
*   **Iron Arm (1CP):** (Fight phase) Add 1 to the Strength characteristic of melee weapons (+2 if **Biomancy** is active).
*   **Assail (1CP):** (Shooting phase) One enemy unit within 18" suffers D3 mortal wounds.
*   **Prescient Precision (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One unit gains the [LETHAL HITS] ability (+ [IGNORES COVER] if **Divination** is active).
