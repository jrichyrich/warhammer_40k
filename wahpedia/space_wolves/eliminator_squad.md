# Datasheet: Eliminator Squad

Eliminator Squads are Phobos-armored snipers who excel at precision assassination and long-range suppression. Utilizing advanced bolt sniper rifles or las fusils, they can eliminate high-value targets with cold, calculated efficiency. Their tactical training and specialized equipment make them a terrifying presence on the battlefield, able to strike from the shadows and vanish before the enemy can react.

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Mark the Target**
Each time this unit **Remains Stationary**, until the start of your next Movement phase, ranged weapons equipped by models in this unit have the **[DEVASTATING WOUNDS]** ability.

### **Reposition Under Covering Fire**
In your Shooting phase, after this unit has shot, if the **ELIMINATOR SERGEANT** is equipped with an **instigator bolt carbine**, this unit can make a Normal move of up to 6" but cannot declare a charge this turn.
