# Datasheet: Eradicator Squad

Eradicator Squads are the premier anti-tank infantry of the Space Marines, specialized in melting the toughest vehicles and monsters with high-intensity melta fire. Armed with a variety of melta weapons, they are trained to identify and exploit the structural weaknesses of their targets, ensuring total obliteration. Their Gravis armor provides the necessary durability to get close to their prey and deliver their devastating payload.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Total Obliteration**
Each time a model in this unit makes a ranged attack that targets a **MONSTER** or **VEHICLE** unit, you can re-roll the Hit roll, you can re-roll the Wound roll, and you can re-roll the Damage roll.

## Unit Composition

*   2-5 Eradicators
*   Every model is equipped with: melta rifle, bolt pistol, close combat weapon.
