# Datasheet: Brutalis Dreadnought

The Brutalis Dreadnought is a melee-focused variant of the Redemptor-class chassis. It is designed to crash into enemy lines and cause maximum destruction in close quarters, utilizing its massive talons or fists to tear through infantry and vehicles alike.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D3**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Brutalis Charge**
Each time this model finishes a Charge move, select one enemy unit within Engagement Range of it and roll one D6: on a 2-3, that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds; on a 4-5, that enemy unit suffers 3 mortal wounds; on a 6, that enemy unit suffers D3+3 mortal wounds.

### **Damaged: 1-4 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-4 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
