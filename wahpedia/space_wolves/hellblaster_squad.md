# Datasheet: Hellblaster Squad

Hellblaster Squads are specialized fire-support units that wield devastating plasma weaponry. They are trained to unleash a focused hail of energy that can melt through armor and vaporize infantry alike. While their weapons are high-risk, the hard-won experience of the Hellblasters allows them to maximize their destructive potential, often sacrificing themselves to ensure the mission's success.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **For the Chapter!**
Each time a model in this unit is destroyed, roll one D6: on a 3+, do not remove it from play. The destroyed model can shoot after the attacking model’s unit has finished making its attacks, and is then removed from play. If this model was destroyed by a **[HAZARDOUS]** weapon, it automatically passes the check and is removed from play after it has shot.

## Unit Composition

*   Every model is equipped with: plasma incinerator, bolt pistol, close combat weapon.
