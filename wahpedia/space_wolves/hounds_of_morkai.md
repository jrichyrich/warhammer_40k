# Datasheet: Hounds of Morkai

Hounds of Morkai are specialized Reivers, tasked with hunting down and eliminating enemy Psykers. They utilize specialized equipment and tactical training to disrupt the warp and strike down their prey with cold, calculated precision. In battle, they are a terrifying force, their howls causing even the most powerful Psykers to falter.

## Abilities
### **Core**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Morkai’s Howl**
In your Shooting phase, you can select one enemy unit within 12" of this unit. That unit must take a Battle-shock test, subtracting 1 from that test if it has the **PSYKER** keyword. If that test is failed, that unit is stunned until the end of your next turn; while a unit is stunned, subtract 1 from the Hit rolls of its psychic attacks.

### **Morkai’s Protection**
This unit has a 4+ invulnerable save against psychic attacks.
