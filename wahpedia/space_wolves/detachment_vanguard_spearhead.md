# Detachment: Vanguard Spearhead

The Vanguard Spearhead is a formation that emphasizes stealth, infiltration, and specialized combat tactics. It is characterized by its ability to strike from the shadows and use advanced technology to outmanoeuvre the enemy.

## Detachment Rule: Shadow Masters
Each time a ranged attack targets an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army that is more than 12" away, subtract 1 from the Hit roll and that unit has the Benefit of Cover.

## Enhancements
*   **The Blade Driven Deep (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES INFANTRY** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, that unit has the [INFILTRATORS] ability.
*   **Ghostweave Cloak (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer has the [STEALTH] and [LONE OPERATIVE] abilities.
*   **Execute and Redeploy (20 pts):** **PHOBOS** model only. After the bearer's unit has shot, it can make a Normal move of up to 6" but cannot declare a charge this turn.
*   **Shadow War Veteran (30 pts):** **PHOBOS** model only. Aura: While an enemy unit within 12" of the bearer is targeted by a Stratagem, increase the cost of that usage by 1CP.

## Stratagems
*   **A Deadly Prize (1CP):** (Movement phase) Sabotage one objective marker you control; until the end of the battle, any enemy unit that ends a move within range suffers D3 mortal wounds (or 3 mortal wounds on a 4+).
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Surgical Strikes (2CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) Melee weapons gain the [PRECISION] ability.
*   **Strike from the Shadows (1CP):** (Shooting phase) Add 1 to the BS and AP characteristic of ranged weapons if the target is more than 12" away; if the target is hit, it must take a Battle-shock test.
*   **Calculated Feint (1CP):** (Enemy Charge phase) When an enemy unit declares a charge, your unit can move D6" (or 6" if it is **PHOBOS** or **SCOUT**).
*   **Guerrilla Tactics (1CP):** (Movement phase) Remove up to two units from the battlefield and place them into Strategic Reserves.
