# Detachment: Anvil Siege Force

The Anvil Siege Force is a formation that specializes in holding key positions and unleashing devastating firepower from a stationary position. It emphasizes the resilience and relentless firepower of the Adeptus Astartes.

## Detachment Rule: Shield of the Imperium
Each time a model in an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army makes a ranged attack, that attack has the [HEAVY] ability. If it already has that ability, add 1 to the Wound roll for that attack if the unit **Remained Stationary** this turn.

## Enhancements
*   **Indomitable Fury (20 pts):** **GRAVIS** model only. The first time the bearer is destroyed, roll one D6: on a 2+, set it back up as close as possible to where it was destroyed, with all its wounds remaining.
*   **Fleet Commander (15 pts):** **CAPTAIN** model only. Once per battle, in your Command phase, select one objective marker. Each enemy unit within 6" of that marker suffers D3 mortal wounds (or 3 mortal wounds on a 4+).
*   **Stoic Defender (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, models in that unit have the Feel No Pain 6+ ability while within range of an objective marker you control. In addition, that unit's OC is halved (rounding up) instead of being 0 while Battle-shocked.
*   **Architect of War (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, ranged weapons equipped by models in that unit have the [IGNORES COVER] ability.

## Stratagems
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Rigid Discipline (1CP):** (Fight phase) At the end of the phase, a unit that is not within Engagement Range of any enemy units can move up to 6" towards your deployment zone or an objective marker.
*   **Not One Backwards Step (1CP):** (Command phase) Select one unit within range of an objective marker; until the start of your next Command phase, double its Objective Control characteristic but it cannot move for any reason.
*   **No Threat Too Great (2CP):** (Shooting phase) Re-roll Wound rolls against **MONSTERS** or **VEHICLES** for one unit.
*   **Battle Drill Recall (1CP):** (Shooting phase) Ranged weapons gain [SUSTAINED HITS 1]; Critical Hits on 5+ if the unit **Remained Stationary**.
*   **Hail of Vengeance (2CP):** (Enemy Shooting phase) After an enemy unit resolves its attacks and a model in your unit is destroyed, your unit can shoot as if it were your Shooting phase.
