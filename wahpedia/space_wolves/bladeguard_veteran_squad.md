# Datasheet: Bladeguard Veteran Squad

Bladeguard Veterans are elite melee duelists who serve as the ultimate guardians of the Chapter's honor. Equipped with master-crafted power weapons and ornate storm shields, they are nearly immovable anchors on the battlefield, able to weather any storm and strike back with lethal precision. Their presence in a mission is a symbol of the Chapter's resilience and their unwavering commitment to the defense of the Imperium.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Bladeguard**
At the start of the Fight phase, select one of the following to apply to models in this unit until the end of the phase:
*   **Swords of the Chapter:** Each time a model in this unit makes a melee attack, re-roll a Hit roll of 1.
*   **Shields of the Chapter:** Each time an invulnerable save roll is made for a model in this unit, re-roll an invulnerable save roll of 1.

## Wargear Abilities
### **Storm Shield**
The bearer has a 4+ invulnerable save.

## Unit Composition
*   1 Bladeguard Veteran Sergeant
*   2-5 Bladeguard Veterans
*   Every model is equipped with: heavy bolt pistol, master-crafted power weapon, storm shield.
