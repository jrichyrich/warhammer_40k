# Datasheet: Wolf Scouts

Unlike the newly inducted Scouts of other Chapters, Wolf Scouts are veteran warriors who have chosen a life of solitary duty and infiltration. They are masters of the wilderness and of the urban sprawl, able to move unseen and unheard through the most hostile environments. Their specialized training and their hard-won experience make them exceptionally effective at disruption and assassination.

### **Deadly Stalkers**
Each time a model in this unit makes an attack that targets an enemy unit, if there are no other units from your opponent's army within 6" of that enemy unit, add 1 to the Wound roll for that attack.

## Wargear Options
*   Any number of models can each have their bolt pistol and combat blade replaced with 1 Astartes shotgun and 1 combat blade.
*   Any number of models can each have their bolt pistol and combat blade replaced with 1 sniper rifle and 1 combat blade.
*   1 model can have its bolt pistol and combat blade replaced with one of the following: 1 heavy bolter, 1 missile launcher.
