# Datasheet: Wolf Scouts

Unlike the newly inducted Scouts of other Chapters, Wolf Scouts are veteran warriors who have chosen a life of solitary duty and infiltration. They are masters of the wilderness and of the urban sprawl, able to move unseen and unheard through the most hostile environments. Their specialized training and their hard-won experience make them exceptionally effective at disruption and assassination.
