# Datasheet: Impulsor

The Impulsor is a fast, lightly armored grav-transport designed to deliver small squads of Space Marines directly to objectives. Utilizing advanced repulsor technology, it can hover over obstacles while its passengers provide fire support through its open top. Its tactical flexibility and speed make it an essential asset for any mission requiring rapid deployment and area control.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D3**
*   **Hover**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Assault Vehicle**
Units can disembark from this transport after it has **Advanced**. Units that do so count as having made a Normal move and are not eligible to declare a charge this turn.

### **Firing Deck 6**
In your Shooting phase, you can select up to 6 models embarked in this transport. For each model selected, you can select one ranged weapon equipped by that model; this transport model is treated as being equipped with those weapons until the end of the phase.

## Wargear Abilities
### **Shield Dome**
This model has a 5+ invulnerable save.
### **Orbital Comms Array**
Each time you target a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit within 6" of this model with a Stratagem, roll one D6: on a 5+, you gain 1CP.
