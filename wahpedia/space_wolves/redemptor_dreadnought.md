# Datasheet: Redemptor Dreadnought

The Redemptor Dreadnought is a modern, powerful combat walker that excels in a variety of roles. It combines heavy armor with devastating weaponry, making it a formidable presence on the battlefield. Its advanced design allows it to weather significant punishment while delivering punishing return fire or crushing enemies in melee.
