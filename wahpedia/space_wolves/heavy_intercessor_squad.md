# Datasheet: Heavy Intercessor Squad

Heavy Intercessor Squads are Gravis-armored units built for extreme durability and long-range fire support. They excel at holding key positions and weather significant punishment while unleashing a steady hail of heavy bolt fire. Their specialized training and advanced armor make them a formidable anchor for any Space Marine mission.

### **Unyielding in the Face of the Foe**
While this unit is within range of an objective marker you control, add 1 to armor saving throws made for models in this unit against attacks with a Damage characteristic of 1.
