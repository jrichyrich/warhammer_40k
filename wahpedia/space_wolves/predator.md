# Datasheet: Predator

The Predator is a versatile main battle tank used by the Space Marines to provide mobile fire support and anti-armor capability. Built on the Rhino chassis, it features improved armor and a powerful turret-mounted primary weapon system. Whether configured as a Destructor for anti-infantry suppression or an Annihilator for hunting enemy vehicles, it is a reliable and effective asset on any battlefield.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D3**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Predator Destructor**
Each time this model makes a ranged attack that targets an **INFANTRY** unit, improve the Armour Penetration characteristic of that attack by 1.

### **Predator Annihilator**
Each time this model makes a ranged attack that targets a **MONSTER** or **VEHICLE** unit, you can re-roll a Damage roll of 1.

### **Damaged: 1-4 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-4 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
