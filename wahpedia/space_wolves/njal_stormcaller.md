# Datasheet: Njal Stormcaller

Njal Stormcaller, the High Rune Priest of the Space Wolves, is a master of the psychic arts. He is able to summon devastating storms of ice and lightning to destroy his enemies and protect his allies. He is a warrior of legend, whose name is whispered in fear by all who oppose the Imperium.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Stormcaller (Psychic)**
While this model is leading a unit, that unit has the **[STEALTH]** ability.

### **Wind Walker (Psychic)**
While this model is leading a unit, ranged weapons equipped by models in that unit have the **[ASSAULT]** ability. In addition, each time that unit Advances, do not roll; instead, add 6" to the Move characteristic of models in that unit for that move.

### **Tempest’s Wrath (Psychic)**
At the end of your Shooting phase, select one enemy unit (excluding **MONSTERS** and **VEHICLES**) that was hit by an attack made with this model’s **Living Lightning** this phase. Until the start of your next Shooting phase, subtract 6" from the Range characteristic of ranged weapons equipped by models in that unit (to a minimum of 12").
