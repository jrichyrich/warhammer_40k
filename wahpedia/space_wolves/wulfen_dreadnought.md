# Datasheet: Wulfen Dreadnought

Wulfen Dreadnoughts are ancient warriors whose minds have been consumed by the Curse of the Wulfen, yet whose broken bodies have been interred within the sarcophagus of a Dreadnought. They are engine of pure rage, charging into the fray with animalistic ferocity and tearing their enemies apart with massive claws and axes.

To do so, roll one D6, adding 2 to the result: this model can be moved a number of inches up to the result, but must finish that move as close as possible to the closest enemy unit (excluding AIRCRAFT), When doing so, this model can be moved within Engagement Range of that enemy unit. Each model can only make one Bestial Rage move per phase.
