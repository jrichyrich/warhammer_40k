# Datasheet: Wulfen Dreadnought

Wulfen Dreadnoughts are ancient warriors whose minds have been consumed by the Curse of the Wulfen, yet whose broken bodies have been interred within the sarcophagus of a Dreadnought. They are engine of pure rage, charging into the fray with animalistic ferocity and tearing their enemies apart with massive claws and axes.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise 1**
*   **Feel No Pain 6+**

### **Bestial Rage**
Each time this model is selected to fight, select one enemy unit within Engagement Range and roll one D6 (add 2 to the result if this model made a Charge move this turn): on a 4-5, that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds; on a 6+, that enemy unit suffers D3+3 mortal wounds.

### **Violent Fury**
If this model is equipped with two melee weapons, those weapons have the **[TWIN-LINKED]** ability.

### **Blizzard Shield (Wargear)**
The bearer has a 4+ invulnerable save.
