# Datasheet: Librarian

Librarians are powerful psykers who have mastered the arts of the warp to empower their brothers and devastate their foes. They are able to summon protective fields of energy and disrupt the psychic efforts of their enemies, providing an essential layer of defense for any Space Marine mission. Their tactical training and specialized equipment make them a formidable presence on the battlefield, able to harness the power of the mind to achieve victory.
