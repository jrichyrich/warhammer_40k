# Detachment: Saga of the Great Wolf

This detachment focuses on the heroic deeds of the Space Wolves' leaders and their ability to inspire their packs.

## Detachment Rules
*   **Master of Wolves:** While a **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** is leading a unit, models in that unit have the Feel No Pain 6+ ability.
*   **Howling Onslaught:** Each time a **SPACE WOLVES** unit from your army declares a charge, you can re-roll the Charge roll.

## Enhancements
*   **Black Death (25 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Melee weapons equipped by the bearer have the [ANTI-MONSTER 4+] and [ANTI-VEHICLE 4+] abilities.
*   **The Wolf Plate (20 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. The bearer has a Save characteristic of 2+ and the Feel No Pain 4+ ability against mortal wounds.
*   **Frostfang (15 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Add 1 to the Attacks and Strength characteristics of the bearer’s melee weapons. Each time the bearer makes a melee attack, a successful unmodified Hit roll of 6 scores a Critical Hit.
*   **Morkai’s Teeth (15 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. In your Shooting phase, after the bearer has shot, select one enemy unit that was hit by one or more of those attacks. Until the end of the phase, each time a friendly SPACE WOLVES model makes an attack that targets that enemy unit, add 1 to the Wound roll.

## Stratagems
*   **Cunning of the Wolf (1CP):** (Movement phase) Set up one **Space Wolves Infantry** unit from Strategic Reserves anywhere on the battlefield more than 9" away from any enemy models, even if it is the first battle round.
*   **Relentless Hunters (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One **Space Wolves** unit can re-roll Hit rolls until the end of the phase.
*   **Pack Tactics (1CP):** (Charge phase) One **Space Wolves** unit within 12" of two or more other friendly **Space Wolves** units can re-roll its Charge roll.
*   **Vengeance of the Wolf (2CP):** (Fight phase) Just after an enemy unit has resolved its attacks, one **Space Wolves** unit that had models destroyed can immediately fight.
*   **Bestial Ferocity (1CP):** (Fight phase) One **Space Wolves** unit adds 1 to the Attacks characteristic of its melee weapons.
*   **Go for the Throat (1CP):** (Fight phase) Melee weapons equipped by models in one **Space Wolves** unit have the [LANCE] and [PRECISION] abilities.
