# Datasheet: Ancient

Ancients are veteran standard-bearers of the Space Marines, tasked with carrying the Chapter's most sacred banners into battle. Their presence is a symbol of the Chapter's history and honor, inspiring their brothers to hold their ground and fight with renewed vigor. Clad in ornate armor and wielding powerful melee weapons, they are nearly immovable anchors on the battlefield, able to weather any storm and ensure the success of the mission.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Faction**

### **Astartes Banner**
While this model is leading a unit, add 1 to the Objective Control characteristic of models in that unit.

### **Unbreakable Duty**
While this model is within range of an objective marker you control, or while it is within 6" of the center of the battlefield, it has the **Feel No Pain 4+** ability.
