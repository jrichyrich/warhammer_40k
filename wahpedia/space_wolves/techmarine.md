# Datasheet: Techmarine

Techmarines are the master artisans and machine-savants of the Space Marines, responsible for the maintenance and repair of the Chapter's vehicles and heavy weaponry. They are often interred for centuries, their bodies enhanced with cybernetics to better serve the Omnissiah. In battle, they perform emergency repairs under fire and can unleash a devastating flurry of attacks if their charges are destroyed. Their tactical training and specialized equipment make them a vital asset for any mission involving heavy armor and vehicles.

## Abilities
### **Core**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Blessing of the Omnissiah**
In your Command phase, select one friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES VEHICLE** model within 3" of this model. That **VEHICLE** model regains up to D3 lost wounds and, until the start of your next Command phase, each time that **VEHICLE** model makes a ranged attack, add 1 to the Hit roll.

### **Vengeance of the Omnissiah**
If a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES VEHICLE** model is destroyed within 12" of this model, until the end of the battle, the Attacks characteristic of this model's Omnissian power axe is 7.

### **Lone Operative**
While this model is within 3" of one or more friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES VEHICLE** models, it has the **[LONE OPERATIVE]** ability.
