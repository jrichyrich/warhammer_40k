# Datasheet: Incursor Squad

Incursor Squads are Phobos-armored reconnaissance units that excel at identifying enemy positions and guiding the fire of their brothers. Utilizing advanced multi-spectrum arrays, they can pinpoint targets and improve the accuracy of friendly units. Their tactical training and specialized equipment make them an invaluable asset for any mission requiring precision and coordination.

### **Multi-spectrum Array**
In your Shooting phase, after this unit has shot, select one enemy unit hit by one or more attacks made by this unit this phase. Until the end of the phase, each time a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit makes an attack that targets that enemy unit, add 1 to the Hit roll.

## Wargear Abilities
### **Haywire Mine**
Once per battle, at the start of any phase, if this unit is within 3" of one or more enemy units, you can select one of those enemy units and roll one D6: on a 2+, that enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds (or 2D3 mortal wounds if it is a **VEHICLE**).
