# Detachment: Champions of Fenris

This detachment focuses on the heroic deeds of Space Wolves characters and their units.

## Detachment Rule: Deeds of Worth
At the start of each battle round, you can select one of the following Sagas to be active for your army. If a **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** from your army has completed a deed associated with a Saga, that Saga becomes active for the rest of the battle.

### **Saga of the Warrior Born**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army destroys one or more enemy **CHARACTER** models.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** Melee weapons equipped by models in **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army have the [SUSTAINED HITS 1] ability.

### **Saga of the Beastslayer**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army destroys one or more enemy **MONSTER** or **VEHICLE** models.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** Melee weapons equipped by models in **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army have the [LETHAL HITS] ability.

### **Saga of the Hunter**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army finishes a Charge move.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army are eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which they Advanced or Fell Back.

### **Saga of the Bear**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army is reduced to below half of its starting number of Wounds but is not destroyed.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** Models in **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army have the Feel No Pain 6+ ability.

## Enhancements
*   **Black Death (25 pts):** Melee weapons have [ANTI-MONSTER 4+] and [ANTI-VEHICLE 4+].
*   **Frost Weapon (15 pts):** Add 1 to the Strength and Armour Penetration characteristics of the bearer's melee weapons.
*   **The Pelt of the Balewolf (10 pts):** Each time an enemy unit within 6" of the bearer's unit takes a Battle-shock test, subtract 1 from that test. In addition, each time an attack targets the bearer's unit, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
*   **Wolf Tail Talisman (20 pts):** Each time an attack is allocated to the bearer, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack.

## Stratagems
*   **Go for the Throat (1CP):** Melee weapons gain [LANCE]; if a Saga is completed, gain an extra -1 AP.
*   **Warrior Pride (1CP):** Target unit is eligible to declare a charge even if it Advanced; if a Saga is completed, add 1 to Wound rolls.
*   **Relentless Assault (1CP):** Fall Back and still shoot/charge.
*   **Death Grip (1CP):** Re-roll Hit rolls in melee.
*   **Savage Strike (1CP):** +1 to Wound in melee.
*   **Honour of the Chapter (1CP):** +1 Attack in melee.
