# Datasheet: Grey Hunters

Grey Hunters are the experienced backbone of the Space Wolves. They are versatile warriors, equally proficient in ranged firefights and bloody melee. Their tactical flexibility and hard-won experience make them one of the most reliable units in the Chapter, able to adapt to any battlefield situation.

## Stats
| M | T | Sv | W | Ld | OC |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 6" | 4 | 3+ | 2 | 6+ | 2 |

## Ranged Weapons
*   **Boltgun:** 24" | A 2 | BS 3+ | S 4 | AP 0 | D 1
*   **Bolt pistol:** 12" | A 1 | BS 3+ | S 4 | AP 0 | D 1 [PISTOL]

## Melee Weapon
*   **Astartes chainsword:** Melee | A 3 | WS 3+ | S 4 | AP -1 | D 1

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Cunning Hunters**
This unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it **Advanced** or **Fell Back**.

## Wargear Options
*   For every 5 models in this unit, up to 2 models can each have their boltgun replaced with one of the following: 1 flamer, 1 meltagun, 1 plasma gun.
*   The Pack Leader can have his boltgun replaced with one of the following: 1 power weapon, 1 power fist.

## Unit Composition
*   1 Grey Hunter Pack Leader
*   4-9 Grey Hunters
*   Every model is equipped with: boltgun, bolt pistol, Astartes chainsword.
