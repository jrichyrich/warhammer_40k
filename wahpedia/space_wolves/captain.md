# Datasheet: Captain

The Captain is the quintessential leader of a Space Marine company, a master tactician and a formidable warrior. Whether leading from the front with a power sword or coordinating strikes from the rear, his presence inspires his brothers to greater deeds of heroism. His tactical flexibility and specialized training allow him to adapt to any battlefield situation, ensuring the success of the mission.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Rites of Battle**
Once per battle round, one unit from your army with this ability can be targeted by a Stratagem for 0CP, even if another unit from your army has already been targeted by that Stratagem this phase.

### **Finest Hour**
Once per battle, at the start of the Fight phase, this model can use this ability. If it does, until the end of the phase, add 3 to the Attacks characteristic of melee weapons equipped by this model and those weapons have the **[DEVASTATING WOUNDS]** ability.
