# Boarding Actions: Space Wolves

Boarding Actions are close-quarters engagements in the cramped corridors of space hulks and derelict ships. Space Wolves excel in these environments, where their melee prowess is most effective.

## Mustering a Boarding Patrol: Space Wolves
To build a Space Wolves Boarding Patrol, you must follow these specific 10th Edition constraints:

*   **Points Limit:** Exactly 500 points.
*   **Unit Selection:** Select units from the following list:
    *   **Characters (Max 1):** Ragnar Blackmane, Logan Grimnar, Arjac Rockfist, or a standard Captain/Librarian/Chaplain.
    *   **Core Units:** Grey Hunters, Blood Claws, Wolf Guard, Wolf Guard Terminators, Hounds of Morkai, Wulfen.
*   **No Vehicles or Monsters:** You cannot take vehicles, Thunderwolf Cavalry, or monsters.
*   **Leaders:** In Boarding Actions, **Leaders do not attach to units**. They act as independent models.

## Rules Adaptations
Several core rules are adapted for the cramped environment of a space hulk:

1.  **Battlefield Command (1CP Stratagem):** Allows a Character to grant their "While leading a unit" abilities to a friendly unit within 6".
2.  **Visibility & Shooting:** Walls and **closed hatchways** block Line of Sight completely. You can only allocate hits to models actually visible to the attacker.
3.  **Hatchways:** Models within 1" can attempt to open/close hatchways at the end of the Movement phase. Roll-off (adding Toughness) if an enemy is on the other side.
4.  **Movement:** FLY is heavily restricted; models cannot move over other models as if they weren't there. Movement is capped at 9".
5.  **Tactical Maneuvers:** Units can perform maneuvers like **Secure Site** (Sticky Objectives) instead of shooting/charging.

## **1. Boarding Strike Detachment**
*Standard formation for aggressive infantry.*

### **Detachment Rule: Drive Home the Blade**
Ranged weapons gain **[SUSTAINED HITS 1]** if models are within 1" of an open hatchway and the target is on the opposite side.

### **Stratagems**
*   **Ceramite Bulwark (1 CP):** **BATTLELINE** unit gains +1 Attack and +1 Strength vs enemies across a hatchway.
*   **Vox-Amplified Roar (1 CP):** Force a Battle-shock test on a unit being charged.
*   **In the Emperor’s Name (1 CP):** Unit on an objective gains a **5+ Feel No Pain**.

---

## **2. Pilum Strike Team Detachment**
*High mobility and hit-and-fade tactics.*

### **Detachment Rule: Relentless Salvoes**
Units are eligible to shoot in a turn in which they **Advanced** or **Fell Back**.

### **Stratagems**
*   **Knife Work (1 CP):** Improve melee Strength and AP by 1.
*   **Master Marksman (1 CP):** Ranged weapons gain **[DEVASTATING WOUNDS]**.
*   **Enhanced Efficiency (1 CP):** After shooting, unit can make a Normal Move of up to 6".

---

## **3. Terminator Assault Detachment**
*Elite spearhead with heavy armor.*

### **Detachment Rule: Sacred Oath**
Once per battle, **Oath of Moment** target grants re-roll Wound rolls in addition to Hit rolls. **TERMINATOR** units are **BATTLELINE**.

### **Stratagems**
*   **Teleport Strike (1 CP):** Deep Strike within 6" of an enemy (cannot charge that turn).
*   **Fury of the First (1 CP):** +1 to Hit for a **TERMINATOR** unit.
*   **Point-Blank Overwatch (1 CP):** Use Overwatch on a **5+**.
