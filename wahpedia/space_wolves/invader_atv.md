# Datasheet: Invader ATV

The Invader ATV is a fast and durable buggy designed to support Outrider squads and provide rapid fire support where it is needed most. Utilizing advanced suspension and a powerful engine, it can navigate the most challenging terrain with ease, while its versatile weapon systems allow it to engage a variety of threats. Its role is one of agile and aggressive support, often acting as an escort for mounted formations and providing a mobile base of operations for the mission.

### **Outrider Escort**
