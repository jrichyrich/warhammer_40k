# Datasheet: Firestrike Servo-turret

The Firestrike Servo-turret is a semi-stationary defensive platform designed to provide accurate and sustained fire against enemy infantry and armor. Utilizing advanced targeting protocols, it can prioritize and eliminate threats with exceptional precision, often acting as a cornerstone for defensive positions. Its rugged construction and powerful weapon systems make it a reliable and effective asset for any mission requiring area denial and suppression.
