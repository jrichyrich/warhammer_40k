# Detachment: Orbital Assault Force

The Orbital Assault Force is a formation that specializes in rapid deployment and orbital strikes. It is characterized by its ability to strike from above and disrupt the enemy's battle lines.

## Detachment Rule: Rapid-drop Deployment
At the start of the battle, you can select up to two (or up to four in a **STRIKE FORCE** battle) friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units and grant them the [DEEP STRIKE] ability. In addition, each time a unit that was set up on the battlefield this turn makes an attack, you can re-roll Wound rolls of 1; if that unit disembarked from a **DROP POD** this turn, you can re-roll Hit rolls of 1 as well.

## Enhancements
*   **Laurels of Thunder (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer's unit can re-roll its Charge roll on the turn it was set up on the battlefield.
*   **Veteran of the Vanguard (20 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer's unit has the [SCOUTS 6"] ability.
*   **Orbital Uplink Reliquary (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. At the end of the Deployment step, you can select up to three friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units and redeploy them. This can include placing them into Strategic Reserves.
*   **Dedicated Gunship (15 pts):** **TERMINATOR** model only. Once per battle, at the end of your opponent's turn, you can remove the bearer's unit from the battlefield and place it into Strategic Reserves.

## Stratagems
*   **Suppression Strafing (1CP):** (Shooting phase) One enemy unit is suppressed (-1 from its Hit rolls and it must take a Battle-shock test).
*   **Tactical Decapitation (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One unit's attacks have the [PRECISION] ability and add 1 to its Hit rolls if targeting a **CHARACTER** unit.
*   **Shock Onslaught (1CP):** (Fight phase) One unit can make a 6" Pile-in and 6" Consolidation move until the end of the phase.
*   **Auto-sense Coordination (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) One unit gains the [LETHAL HITS] or [SUSTAINED HITS 1] ability on the turn it was set up.
*   **Blind Screen (1CP):** (Enemy Shooting phase) One unit near a **SMOKE VEHICLE** or **DROP POD** gains the [STEALTH] ability and the Benefit of Cover.
*   **Onward for the Emperor (1CP):** (Enemy Fight phase) At the end of the phase, one unit can embark into a friendly **TRANSPORT** if all its models are within 3".
