# Datasheet: Infernus Squad

Infernus Squads are specialized fire-support units that wield powerful pyreblasters, designed to flush enemies out of cover and force morale checks. Armed with these devastating flamethrowers, they are adept at securing territory and disrupting enemy formations. Their specialized training allows them to maximize the psychological and physical impact of their weapons, often causing entire units to falter under the intense heat and pressure.

## Abilities
### **Faction**

### **Purge the Foe**
In your Shooting phase, after this unit has shot, select one enemy **INFANTRY** unit hit by one or more attacks made by this unit this phase. That enemy unit must take a Battle-shock test, subtracting 1 from that test.
