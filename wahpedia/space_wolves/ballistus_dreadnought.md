# Datasheet: Ballistus Dreadnought

The Ballistus Dreadnought is a specialized fire-support variant of the Redemptor-class chassis. It is designed to engage enemy armor and heavy fortifications from long range, utilizing a powerful lascannon and a versatile missile launcher to eliminate threats before they can reach the Space Marine lines.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D3**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Ballistus Strike**
Each time this model makes a ranged attack against a unit that is **not** Below Half-strength, you can re-roll the Hit roll.

### **Damaged: 1-4 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-4 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
