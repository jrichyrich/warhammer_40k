# Datasheet: Thunderwolf Cavalry

The Thunderwolf Cavalry are the elite of the Space Wolves, warriors who have tamed the massive and ferocious Thunderwolves of Fenris. They charge into battle with terrifying speed, their mounts' teeth and claws tearing through armor and flesh alike as the riders strike with their own weapons.

## Abilities
### **Faction**

### **Thunderous Charge**
Each time a model in this unit makes a melee attack with its **Wolf Guard weapon**, if it made a Charge move this turn, add 1 to the Damage characteristic of that attack.
