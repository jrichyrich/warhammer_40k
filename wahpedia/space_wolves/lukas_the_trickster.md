# Datasheet: Lukas the Trickster

Lukas the Trickster is a rogue among the Space Wolves, a warrior whose cunning and irreverence are as legendary as his battlefield exploits. He is a master of psychological warfare, using his wits and a collection of strange artifacts to sow confusion and fear among his enemies.
