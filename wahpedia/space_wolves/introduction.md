# Introduction: Space Wolves

With blood-chilling howls the warriors of the Space Wolves hurl themselves into battle, hacking and tearing with a primal, predatory ferocity. Mighty warriors whose deeds and sagas stretch back to the dawn of the Imperium, the Sons of Russ are amongst Humanity’s most redoubtable defenders.

The Space Wolves, or **Vlka Fenryka**, are a Chapter of Space Marines known for their fierce, independent nature and their deep-rooted cultural heritage from the death world of Fenris. Unlike many other Chapters that adhere strictly to the **Codex Astartes**, the Space Wolves maintain their own unique organizational structure, traditions, and tactics, often characterized by their pack-like hunting methods and the presence of giant Fenrisian Wolves in their ranks.

## Lore Overview
The Sons of Russ are renowned for their bravery and their commitment to protecting the Imperium, even if it means defying authority. They are the emperors' executioners, a title they wear with both pride and a grim sense of duty. Their sagas are sung in the Great Hall of the Fang, their mountain fortress on Fenris, recording the legendary deeds of heroes like Logan Grimnar, Ragnar Blackmane, and Bjorn the Fell-handed.
