# Datasheet: Repulsor

The Repulsor is a heavy battle transport that combines immense firepower with advanced repulsor technology. It is designed to deliver full squads of Space Marines directly into the heart of the enemy's lines, while providing overwhelming fire support from its massive array of anti-infantry weapons. Its advanced sensors and systems allow it to perform emergency combat embarkations, making it an exceptionally flexible and durable asset.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D6**
*   **Hover**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Emergency Combat Embarkation**
Once per turn, in your opponent’s Charge phase, after an enemy unit has selected targets for its charge but before it moves, if this transport is not within Engagement Range of any enemy units, you can select one friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES INFANTRY** unit within 3" of this transport that was selected as a target of that charge. That unit can embark within this transport.

### **Damaged: 1-5 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-5 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
