## Introduction
With blood-chilling howls the warriors of the Space Wolves hurl themselves into battle, hacking and tearing with a primal, predatory ferocity. Mighty warriors whose deeds and sagas stretch back to the dawn of the Imperium, the Sons of Russ are amongst Humanity’s most redoubtable defenders.

The Space Wolves, or **Vlka Fenryka**, are a Chapter of Space Marines known for their fierce, independent nature and their deep-rooted cultural heritage from the death world of Fenris. Unlike many other Chapters that adhere strictly to the **Codex Astartes**, the Space Wolves maintain their own unique organizational structure, traditions, and tactics, often characterized by their pack-like hunting methods and the presence of giant Fenrisian Wolves in their ranks.

### Lore Overview
The Sons of Russ are renowned for their bravery and their commitment to protecting the Imperium, even if it means defying authority. They are the emperors' executioners, a title they wear with both pride and a grim sense of duty. Their sagas are sung in the Great Hall of the Fang, their mountain fortress on Fenris, recording the legendary deeds of heroes like Logan Grimnar, Ragnar Blackmane, and Bjorn the Fell-handed.

## Army Rules
The Space Wolves, being a specialized Chapter of the Adeptus Astartes, have access to both the core **Oath of Moment** rule and their unique Chapter-specific mechanics.

### Oath of Moment
At the start of your Command phase, select one enemy unit. Until your next Command phase, each time a model with this ability makes an attack that targets that unit, you can re-roll the Hit roll.

### Curse of the Wulfen
Units within 6" of a **Space Wolves Character** (or 12" of a **Wolf Priest**) gain a bonus to their Objective Control (OC) characteristic (+1 for **Infantry**, +3 for **Vehicles**), provided they are not **Battle-shocked**.

### Sagas
Each Space Wolves detachment includes a specific "Saga" (a heroic feat). Once the conditions of the Saga are met during battle, additional powerful effects are unlocked for the remainder of the game.

### Sons of Russ (Restrictions)
A Space Wolves army cannot include the following units:
*   **Apothecaries**
*   **Devastator Squads**
*   **Tactical Squads**

It also cannot include units from any other Space Marine Chapter (e.g., **Ultramarines**, **Blood Angels**, etc.).

## Detachments

### Champions of Fenris
This detachment focuses on the heroic deeds of Space Wolves characters and their units.

#### Detachment Rule: Deeds of Worth
At the start of each battle round, you can select one of the following Sagas to be active for your army. If a **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** from your army has completed a deed associated with a Saga, that Saga becomes active for the rest of the battle.

##### **Saga of the Warrior Born**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army destroys one or more enemy **CHARACTER** models.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** Melee weapons equipped by models in **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army have the [SUSTAINED HITS 1] ability.

##### **Saga of the Beastslayer**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army destroys one or more enemy **MONSTER** or **VEHICLE** models.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** Melee weapons equipped by models in **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army have the [LETHAL HITS] ability.

##### **Saga of the Hunter**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army finishes a Charge move.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army are eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which they Advanced or Fell Back.

##### **Saga of the Bear**
*   **Deed:** A **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** model from your army is reduced to below half of its starting number of Wounds but is not destroyed.
*   **Saga Active Bonus:** Models in **SPACE WOLVES** units from your army have the Feel No Pain 6+ ability.

#### Enhancements
*   **Black Death (25 pts):** Melee weapons have [ANTI-MONSTER 4+] and [ANTI-VEHICLE 4+].
*   **Frost Weapon (15 pts):** Add 1 to the Strength and Armour Penetration characteristics of the bearer's melee weapons.
*   **The Pelt of the Balewolf (10 pts):** Each time an enemy unit within 6" of the bearer's unit takes a Battle-shock test, subtract 1 from that test. In addition, each time an attack targets the bearer's unit, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
*   **Wolf Tail Talisman (20 pts):** Each time an attack is allocated to the bearer, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack.

#### Stratagems
*   **Go for the Throat (1CP):** Melee weapons gain [LANCE]; if a Saga is completed, gain an extra -1 AP.
*   **Warrior Pride (1CP):** Target unit is eligible to declare a charge even if it Advanced; if a Saga is completed, add 1 to Wound rolls.
*   **Relentless Assault (1CP):** Fall Back and still shoot/charge.
*   **Death Grip (1CP):** Re-roll Hit rolls in melee.
*   **Savage Strike (1CP):** +1 to Wound in melee.
*   **Honour of the Chapter (1CP):** +1 Attack in melee.

### Saga of the Hunter
This detachment focuses on lightning speed and shock assaults, utilizing outflanking and infiltration.

#### Detachment Rule: Pack’s Quarry
At their Wolf Lord’s command, the Space Wolves advance upon their prey with lupine hunger, singling out the weak and overwhelming the strong with concentrated force and furious aggression.

*   **Effect:** Each time a model in a **SPACE WOLVES** unit from your army makes a melee attack that targets an enemy unit, if that enemy unit is within Engagement Range of one or more other **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units from your army, or if the attacking unit contains more models than that enemy unit:
    *   Add 1 to the Hit roll.
    *   If your **Saga is completed**, add 1 to the Wound roll as well.

##### **Saga of the Hunter (Completion Condition)**
*   At the start of the first battle round, your Quarry tally is 0.
*   Each time an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army fights, after all of those attacks have been resolved, add 1 to your Quarry tally for each enemy unit destroyed by those attacks.
*   Once your Quarry tally is equal to or greater than the number shown below (depending on the battle size), your Saga is completed:
    *   **Incursion:** 2
    *   **Strike Force:** 3
    *   **Onslaught:** 4

#### Enhancements
*   **Swift Hunter (20 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Models in the bearer’s unit have the **Scouts 7"** ability.
*   **Fenrisian Grit (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. The bearer has the **Feel No Pain 4+** ability.
*   **Wolf Master (5 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. In your Command phase, select one friendly **SPACE WOLVES** unit within 9" of the bearer. Until the start of your next Command phase, teeth and claws and Tyrnak and Fenrir weapons equipped by models in that unit have the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability.
*   **Feral Rage (10 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of melee weapons equipped by the bearer. Each time the bearer ends a Charge move, until the end of the turn, add an additional 1 to the Attacks characteristic of those weapons.

#### Stratagems
*   **Hunters’ Trail (1CP):** (Fight phase) Pile-in or Consolidation moves can be up to 6" instead of 3".
*   **Territorial Advantage (1CP):** (Fight phase) Objective marker remains under your control even if you move away.
*   **Overwhelming Onslaught (1CP):** (Fight phase) Enemy unit subtracts 1 from Hit rolls.
*   **Chosen Prey (1CP):** (Movement phase) Unit is eligible to shoot and charge in the same turn it Fell Back.
*   **Bounding Advance (1CP):** (Movement or Charge phase) Models can move through other models.
*   **Marked for Destruction (1CP):** (Shooting phase) Re-roll Wound rolls of 1 against a selected enemy unit.

### Saga of the Bold
This detachment emphasizes heroic deeds and champions leading by example.

#### Detachment Rule: Heroes All
Every Space Wolves warrior seeks to forge a saga worthy of retelling. Each time an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army is selected to shoot or fight, apply one of the following when resolving those attacks:
*   **If your Saga is completed:** You can re-roll one Hit roll, one Wound roll, and one Damage roll.
*   **Otherwise:** If that unit is a **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** unit, you can re-roll one Hit roll, one Wound roll, or one Damage roll.

##### **Saga of the Bold (Completion Criteria)**
Each time a **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** unit from your army achieves a **Boast**, it is recorded. Once **three or more different Boasts** have been achieved by units from your army, your Saga is completed.
*   **Your Hide as a Trophy:** That unit destroys your Oath of Moment target.
*   **Slay Them All:** That unit destroys your Oath of Moment target, and that is the second Oath of Moment target destroyed by that unit in this battle.
*   **Overrun Their Position:** At the end of either player’s turn, that unit is wholly within your opponent’s deployment zone.
*   **Hold the Line:** From the second battle round onwards, at the end of your Command phase, that unit is within range of an objective marker you control that is not within your deployment zone.

#### Enhancements
*   **Braggart’s Steel (20 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Add 2 to the Strength characteristic of melee weapons. If unit achieved a Boast, add 1 to Damage.
*   **Skjald (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Gain 1CP each time a **CHARACTER** achieves a Boast.
*   **Hordeslayer (15 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Add 2 to Attacks if more enemy models within 6" (+3 if achieved a Boast).
*   **Thunderwolf’s Fortitude (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Resurrect on a 2+ with 3 wounds.

#### Stratagems
*   **Inspiring Presence (1CP):** (Fight phase) **CHARACTER** unit gains **[LETHAL HITS]**.
*   **Champion’s Guidance (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) **CHARACTER** unit can re-roll Hit rolls.
*   **Birth of a Saga (1CP):** (Command phase) Grant **Wolf Guard Headtaker** or **Terminator Pack Leader** the **CHARACTER** keyword.
*   **Alpha Strike (1CP):** (Charge phase) **CHARACTER** unit can charge after Advancing.
*   **Heroic Resolve (2CP):** (Opponent's Shooting phase) **CHARACTER** unit subtracts 1 from Damage.
*   **Countercharge (2CP):** (End of opponent's Charge phase) **CHARACTER** unit can declare a charge.

### Saga of the Beastslayer
This detachment is specialized in hunting down and slaying the most formidable foes, such as Monsters and Vehicles.

Each time an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model from your army makes an attack, if that attack targets a **CHARACTER**, **MONSTER**, or **VEHICLE** unit, or if your Saga is completed, that attack has the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability.

##### **Saga of the Beastslayer (Completion)**
*   **Setup:** At the start of the first battle round, your Beastslayer tally is 0. Beastslayer target is half the number of opponent's **CHARACTER**, **MONSTER**, and **VEHICLE** units.
*   **Completion:** Once tally reaches target by destroying units with those keywords, Saga is completed.

#### Enhancements

*   **Pelt of the Balewolf (10 pts):** -1 to Hit rolls for attacks targeting the unit.
*   **Frost Weapon (25 pts):** Improve Strength and AP of melee weapons by 1.

#### Stratagems
*   **Morkai’s Teeth (1CP):** Bolt weapons gain **[DEVASTATING WOUNDS]** against **Psyker** units.
*   **Scent of Blood (1CP):** +2 to Charge roll against unit below starting strength.
*   **Relentless Predators (1CP):** Make a 6" move after an enemy ends a move within 9".
*   **Pack Hunters (1CP):** Other units get +1 to Wound against a target in Engagement Range.
*   **Hunter’s Instincts (1CP):** Unit can shoot and charge after Advancing or Falling Back.
*   **Beastslayer’s Fury (2CP):** +1 to Wound and +1 Damage against **Monster** or **Vehicle**.

## Crusade Rules
Space Wolves Crusade rules focus on the "Forging of Legends" and the "Sagas of the Wolf," tracking the heroic deeds of individual warriors.

### The Forging of Legends
*   **Chapter Command:** Assign characters to roles (Wolf Lord, Battle Leader). Promote through ranks: **Blooded**, **Battle-hardened**, **Heroic**, and **Legendary**.
*   **Deeds of Making:** Earn permanent titles (e.g., "Beastslayer") for unique buffs based on performance.

### Oathsworn Campaigns
Bespoke narrative campaigns lasting 3–4 battles to earn **Honour Points**.
*   **Worthy of a Saga:** Individual character renown.
*   **Glory Seekers:** Great Company competition for prestigious kills.
*   **Hunters Unleashed:** Tracking and eliminating high-value targets.

### Lone Wolves
A unique mechanic for warriors who have lost their packs.
*   **The Lone Wolf’s Vow:** Replace a destroyed unit with a **Wolf Guard Battle Leader** model.
*   **Abilities:** He gains **[LONE OPERATIVE]** and **Oath of Vengeance** (re-roll Hit rolls; re-roll Wounds vs Monsters/Vehicles).
*   **Lone Wolf Battle Scars:** Unique scars like **Pack of One** ([INFILTRATORS], [SCOUTS 6"], OC 0) and **An Honourable End** (shoot/charge after Advance).

## Boarding Actions
Close-quarters engagements in space hulks and derelict ships.

### 1. Boarding Strike
Focuses on standard infantry and hatchway control.
*   **Detachment Rule:** **Drive Home the Blade** ([SUSTAINED HITS 1] near open hatchways).
*   **Stratagems:** **Ceramite Bulwark**, **Vox-Amplified Roar**, **In the Emperor’s Name**.

### 2. Pilum Strike Team
Focuses on high mobility and Hounds of Morkai.
*   **Detachment Rule:** **Relentless Salvoes** (Shoot after Advance/Fall Back).
*   **Stratagems:** **Knife Work**, **Master Marksman**, **Enhanced Efficiency**.

### 3. Terminator Assault
Focuses on elite spearheads and heavy armor.
*   **Detachment Rule:** **Sacred Oath** (Re-roll Hit/Wound rolls against Oath target once per battle; Terminators are Battleline).
*   **Stratagems:** **Teleport Strike**, **Fury of the First**, **Point-Blank Overwatch**.
