# Datasheet: Ulrik the Slayer

Ulrik the Slayer is the High Wolf Priest of the Space Wolves, a venerable warrior who has seen countless battles and whose wisdom is sought by all. He is a master of the hunt, able to single out the most powerful enemies and lead his brothers to victory.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Slayer’s Oath**
At the start of the battle, select one of the following keywords: **CHARACTER**, **MONSTER**, or **VEHICLE**. The first time a model in this model’s unit destroys an enemy unit with that keyword, for the remainder of the battle, this model’s unit is treated as having completed the Saga associated with your detachment (if any).

### **Oathbound**
While this model is leading a unit, each time a model in that unit makes a melee attack, add 1 to the Hit roll. If the target of that attack has the keyword you selected for the **Slayer’s Oath** ability, add 1 to the Wound roll as well.
