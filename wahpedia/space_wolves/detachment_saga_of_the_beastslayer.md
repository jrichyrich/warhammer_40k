# Detachment: Saga of the Beastslayer

This detachment is specialized in hunting down and slaying the most formidable foes, such as Monsters and Vehicles.

## Detachment Rule: Legendary Slayers
Some Space Wolves seek only to bring down the most monstrous and deadly foes. With hunting packs of Fenrisian wolves, Thunderwolf Cavalry, and rampaging bands of Wulfen, they seek to hunt down and slay mighty champions, towering monstrosities, and rumbling war machines.

*   **Rule:** Each time an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model from your army makes an attack, if that attack targets a **CHARACTER**, **MONSTER**, or **VEHICLE** unit, or if your Saga is completed (see below), that attack has the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability.

### **Saga of the Beastslayer (Completion)**
*   **Setup:** At the start of the first battle round, your Beastslayer tally is 0. Determine your Beastslayer target by halving the number of units from your opponent’s army (including those embarked within **TRANSPORTS**) that have one or more of the following keywords (rounding up): **CHARACTER**, **MONSTER**, **VEHICLE**.
*   **Progress:** Each time an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army shoots or fights, after all of those attacks have been resolved, add 1 to your Beastslayer tally for each enemy unit with one or more of the following keywords destroyed by those attacks: **CHARACTER**, **MONSTER**, **VEHICLE**.
*   **Completion:** Once your Beastslayer tally is equal to or greater than your Beastslayer target, your Saga is completed.

## Enhancements
*   **Wolf-touched (15 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Add 2" to the Move characteristic of the bearer. In the Declare Battle Formations step, the bearer can be attached to a **WULFEN INFANTRY** unit.
*   **Hunter’s Guile (20 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, that unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced.
*   **Beastslayer’s Trophy (15 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Each time the bearer makes a melee attack that targets a **MONSTER** or **VEHICLE** unit, add 1 to the Wound roll.
*   **Pelt of the Balewolf (10 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Each time a melee attack targets the bearer’s unit, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
*   **Frost Weapon (25 pts):** **SPACE WOLVES** model only. Improve the Strength and Armour Penetration characteristics of the bearer’s melee weapons by 1.

## Stratagems
*   **Morkai’s Teeth (1CP):** (Shooting phase) One **Hounds of Morkai** unit or one **Space Wolves** unit equipped with bolt weapons gains **[DEVASTATING WOUNDS]** against **Psyker** units.
*   **Scent of Blood (1CP):** (Movement phase) One enemy unit that is below its Starting Strength. Until the end of the turn, add 2 to the Charge roll for any **Space Wolves** unit declaring a charge against it.
*   **Relentless Predators (1CP):** (Opponent's Movement phase) Just after an enemy unit ends a move, one **Space Wolves** unit within 9" can make a Normal move of up to 6".
*   **Pack Hunters (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) Select one enemy unit and one **Space Wolves** unit within Engagement Range. All other **Space Wolves** units add 1 to the Wound roll for attacks targeting that enemy unit.
*   **Hunter’s Instincts (1CP):** (Movement phase) One **Space Wolves** unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced or Fell Back.
*   **Beastslayer’s Fury (2CP):** (Fight phase) Melee attacks against a **Monster** or **Vehicle** unit add 1 to the Wound roll and increase Damage by 1.
