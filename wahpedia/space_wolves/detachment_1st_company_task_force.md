# Detachment: 1st Company Task Force

The 1st Company Task Force is a formation that emphasizes the elite status and devastating firepower of the Adeptus Astartes' most veteran warriors. It is characterized by its ability to focus its attacks on the most significant threats.

## Detachment Rule: Extremis-level Threat
Once per battle, in your Command phase, you can select one enemy unit to be your **Extremis-level Threat**. Until the start of your next Command phase, each time a model in an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army makes an attack that targets that unit, you can re-roll the Wound roll.

## Enhancements
*   **The Imperium’s Sword (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of the bearer's melee weapons. Once per battle, in the Fight phase, you can grant +1 Attacks to all models in the bearer's unit until the end of the phase.
*   **Fear Made Manifest (30 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Aura: While an enemy unit is within 6" of the bearer, each time that unit fails a Battle-shock test, one model in that unit is destroyed.
*   **Rites of War (10 pts):** **TERMINATOR** model only. Add 1 to the Objective Control characteristic of the bearer. Once per battle, in your Command phase, you can grant +1 OC to all models in the bearer's unit until the start of your next Command phase.
*   **Iron Resolve (15 pts):** **TERMINATOR** model only. The bearer has the Feel No Pain 5+ ability. Once per battle, in any phase, you can grant a 5+ Feel No Pain to all models in the bearer's unit until the end of the phase.

## Stratagems
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Heroes of the Chapter (1CP):** (Shooting or Fight phase) Add 1 to the Hit rolls for one unit (+1 to the Wound rolls if the unit is below half-strength).
*   **Terrifying Proficiency (1CP):** (Charge phase) After a charge and a kill, force all enemy units within 6" of the unit to take a Battle-shock test.
*   **Duty and Honour (1CP):** (Command phase) One objective marker remains under your control even if you move away from it.
*   **Orbital Teleportarium (1CP):** (Movement phase) Remove one **TERMINATOR** unit from the battlefield and place it into Strategic Reserves for Deep Strike.
*   **Legendary Fortitude (1CP):** (Enemy Charge phase) Subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of attacks targeting the unit that was charged.
