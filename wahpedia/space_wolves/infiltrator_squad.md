# Datasheet: Infiltrator Squad

Infiltrator Squads are specialized Phobos-armored units tasked with board control and backline protection. Utilizing advanced omni-scramblers, they can disrupt enemy teleportation and prevent reinforcements from arriving near their position. Their tactical training and specialized equipment make them an essential asset for any mission requiring stealth and security.
