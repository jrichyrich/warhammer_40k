# Datasheet: Fenrisian Wolves

Fenrisian Wolves are massive, intelligent predators native to the death world of Fenris. They are often tamed by the Space Wolves and fight alongside them in battle, their speed and ferocity making them excellent hunters and harriers.

| 10" | 4 | 6+ | 1 | 8+ | 0 |

## Melee Weapon
*   **Teeth and claws:** Melee | A 2 | WS 4+ | S 4 | AP 0 | D 1

## Abilities
### **Swift Hunters**
This unit is eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced.

### **Predatory Instinct**
Once per turn, when an enemy unit ends a Normal, Advance, or Fall Back move within 9" of this unit, this unit can make a Normal move of up to D6".

### **Hunting Hounds**
While this unit is within 6" of one or more friendly **SPACE WOLVES CHARACTER** models (excluding **WULFEN** models), if this unit is not Battle-shocked, it has an Objective Control characteristic of 1.

## Unit Composition
*   5-10 Fenrisian Wolves
*   Equipped with: teeth and claws.
