# Datasheet: Inceptor Squad

Inceptor Squads are Gravis-armored jump pack units designed for rapid orbital descent and shock assaults. They crash into the battlefield with meteoric force, unleashing a devastating hail of fire from their assault bolters or plasma exterminators. Their advanced armor and jump packs allow them to bypass enemy defenses and strike at the heart of their formations, often arriving where the enemy least expects them.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deep Strike**
*   **Fly**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Meteoric Descent**
Each time this unit is set up on the battlefield using the **Deep Strike** ability, it can be set up anywhere that is more than 3" horizontally away from all enemy models. If it is set up in this way, it is not eligible to declare a charge this turn.

## Unit Composition

*   2-5 Inceptors
*   Every model is equipped with: assault bolters.
