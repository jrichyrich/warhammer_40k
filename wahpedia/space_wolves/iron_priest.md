# Datasheet: Iron Priest

Iron Priests are the master artisans and tech-savants of the Space Wolves. They are responsible for the maintenance and repair of the Chapter's vehicles and heavy weaponry, often venturing into the thick of battle to perform emergency repairs under fire. They are also formidable warriors in their own right, wielding powerful Helfrost weapons and crushing hammers.

### **Gift of the Iron Wolf**
In your Command phase, select one friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES VEHICLE** within 3" of this model. That **VEHICLE** model regains up to D3 lost wounds and, until the start of your next Command phase, select one ranged weapon equipped by that model; that weapon has the **[RAPID FIRE 1]** ability.
