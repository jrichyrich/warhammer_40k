# Datasheet: Krom Dragongaze

Krom Dragongaze, known as the Fierce-eye, is a Wolf Lord of legendary aggression. His piercing stare is said to be as deadly as his axe, Wyrmclaw, and he leads his Great Company from the front, seeking out the most powerful foes to test his mettle against.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **The Fierce-eye**
In your Shooting phase, select one enemy **INFANTRY** unit within 12" of this model and visible to it. That unit must take a Battle-shock test.

### **The Sun Wolf’s Gaze**
While this model is leading a unit, each time a model in that unit makes an attack, add 1 to the Hit roll if that unit is below its Starting Strength. If that unit is **Below Half-strength**, add 1 to the Wound roll as well.
