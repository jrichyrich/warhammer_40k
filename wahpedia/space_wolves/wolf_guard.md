# Datasheet: Wolf Guard

Earning their place through exceptional feats of arms, every Wolf Guard is a heroic warrior chosen by their lord. They act as his council and household companions, and when not tearing apart foes at his side, they form bodyguards around other senior warriors or pursue dangerous missions in their own right.
