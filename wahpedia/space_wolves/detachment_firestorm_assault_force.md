# Detachment: Firestorm Assault Force

The Firestorm Assault Force is a formation that specializes in aggressive, close-quarters combat and the use of short-range weaponry. It is characterized by its ability to move quickly and unleash devastating firepower at close range.

## Detachment Rule: Close-range Eradication
Each time a model in an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army makes a ranged attack against an enemy unit within 12", add 1 to the Strength characteristic of that attack. In addition, ranged weapons equipped by models in that unit have the [ASSAULT] ability.

## Enhancements
*   **Champion of Humanity (10 pts):** **TACTICUS** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, that unit ignores all modifiers to its characteristics and to rolls made for models in that unit.
*   **War-tempered Artifice (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Add 3 to the Strength characteristic of the bearer's melee weapons.
*   **Forged in Battle (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Once per turn, you can change the result of one Hit roll or one Save roll made for the bearer to a 6.
*   **Adamantine Mantle (20 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. Subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of attacks targeting the bearer. This does not apply to attacks with the [MELTA] or [TORRENT] ability.

## Stratagems
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Crucible of Battle (1CP):** (Shooting phase) One unit within 6" of its target adds 1 to its Wound rolls.
*   **Rapid Embarkation (1CP):** (Fight phase) At the end of the phase, one unit can embark into a friendly **TRANSPORT** if all its models are within 3".
*   **Immolation Protocols (2CP):** (Shooting phase) Ranged weapons with the [TORRENT] ability gain the [DEVASTATING WOUNDS] ability.
*   **Onslaught of Fire (1CP):** (Shooting phase) After disembarking, a unit adds 1 to its Hit rolls; if it destroys an enemy unit, that enemy unit must take a Battle-shock test.
*   **Burning Vengeance (1CP):** (Enemy Shooting phase) After a **TRANSPORT** is targeted, models can disembark and shoot as if it were your Shooting phase.
