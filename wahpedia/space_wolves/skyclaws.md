# Datasheet: Skyclaws

Skyclaws are Blood Claws who have been equipped with jump packs, allowing them to leap across the battlefield and crash into their enemies with terrifying force. They are known for their aggressive and often reckless charges, their raw enthusiasm making them a potent force in close-quarters combat.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Headstrong**
Each time this unit declares a charge, if it is not within 6" of one or more friendly **WOLF GUARD** models, you must subtract 1 from the Charge roll.

### **Shock Assault**
Each time a model in this unit makes a melee attack, if this unit made a Charge move this turn, add 1 to the Hit roll.

## Wargear Options
*   Up to 2 models can each have their bolt pistol and Astartes chainsword replaced with one of the following: 1 flamer, 1 meltagun, 1 plasma gun.
*   The Pack Leader can have his Astartes chainsword replaced with one of the following: 1 power weapon, 1 power fist.

## Unit Composition
*   1 Skyclaw Pack Leader
*   4-9 Skyclaws
*   Every model is equipped with: jump pack, bolt pistol, Astartes chainsword.
