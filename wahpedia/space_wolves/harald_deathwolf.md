# Datasheet: Harald Deathwolf

Harald Deathwolf is the Wolf Lord of the Deathwolves, a Great Company known for its mastery of mounted warfare and the use of massive Thunderwolves. He rides his own mount, Icetooth, into the thickest fighting, a master of the charge and a hero of legend among the Sons of Russ.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Lord of the Wolfkin**
While this model is leading a unit, each time a model in that unit makes a melee attack with its **Crushing teeth and claws**, if that unit made a Charge move this turn, that attack has the **[DEVASTATING WOUNDS]** ability.

### **The Mantle of the Icemaw**
While this model is leading a unit, models in that unit have the Feel No Pain 4+ ability against mortal wounds.
