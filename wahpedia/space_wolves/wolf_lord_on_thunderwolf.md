# Datasheet: Wolf Lord on Thunderwolf

Wolf Lords who ride to battle atop massive Thunderwolves are among the most fearsome warriors in the Galaxy. They are masters of the charge, leading their cavalry into the heart of the enemy's lines with a ferocity that is truly terrifying. They are also exceptional leaders, able to inspire their brothers to greater deeds of heroism through their own example.

### **Rites of Battle**
