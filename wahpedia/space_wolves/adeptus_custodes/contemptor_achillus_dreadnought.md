# Datasheet: Contemptor-Achillus Dreadnought

The Contemptor-Achillus Dreadnought is a specialized melee-focused walker of the Adeptus Custodes, designed to lead aggressive shock assaults and eliminate the most formidable enemy targets. Armed with the massive Achillus dreadnought spear and housing a fallen hero of the Ten Thousand, it is a whirlwind of destruction on the battlefield. Its advanced systems and heavy auramite armor allow it to weather significant punishment while delivering punishing return fire or crushing enemies in close quarters. Its role is one of direct and brutal execution, ensuring that no enemy can stand before the golden legion.
