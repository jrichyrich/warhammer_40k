# Datasheet: Vertus Praetors

Vertus Praetors are the mounted elite of the Adeptus Custodes, riding to battle atop ornate Dawneagle jetbikes. They are the Chapter's primary fast attack force, utilizing their extreme speed and maneuverability to strike at the enemy's flanks or perform daring hit-and-run charges. Equipped with Interceptor lances and specialized salvo launchers, they are able to engage and destroy any target, from swarming infantry to the heaviest of armored vehicles. Their role is one of agile and aggressive support, often acting as a spearhead for larger Custodian formations and ensuring the mission's success through their lightning-fast strikes.

### **Quicksilver Execution**
Once per battle, after this unit ends a Normal move, you can select one enemy unit it moved over. Roll one D6 for each model in this unit: for each 2+, that enemy unit suffers 2 mortal wounds.
