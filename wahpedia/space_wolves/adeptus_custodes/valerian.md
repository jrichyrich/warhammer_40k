# Datasheet: Valerian

Valerian, a Shield-Captain of the Palaiologos Chamber, is a warrior-scholar of the Adeptus Custodes. Known for his unwavering stoicism and his deep understanding of history and philosophy, he is a formidable leader and a lethal combatant. Wielding the legendary spear Gnosis, he strikes with a precision and strength that reflects his ten thousand years of training. His role is one of both defender and executor, ensuring the Emperor's will is carried out with cold, calculated efficiency.
