# Datasheet: Knight-Centura

The Knight-Centura is a high-ranking officer of the Sisters of Silence, a veteran warrior and a master of the anti-psychic arts. Her presence on the battlefield is a bane to any psyker, her sheer will and specialized training allowing her to disrupt their powers and lead her sisters with a cold, calculated precision. Wielding a massive executioner greatblade or a powerful witchseeker flamer, she is a lethal combatant, able to bypass enemy defenses and strike at the heart of their command. Her role is one of both tactical coordination and direct execution, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are purged and his servants are shielded from the corruption of the warp.
