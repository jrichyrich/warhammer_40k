# Army Rules: Adeptus Custodes

The Adeptus Custodes utilize specialized combat forms and supernatural resilience to dominate the battlefield.

## Martial Ka’tah
Each time an **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** unit from your army is selected to fight, if it is not **Battle-shocked**, you can select one of the following Ka’tah Stances. Until that unit’s attacks are resolved, that unit benefits from the corresponding ability:

*   **Dacatarai Stance:** Melee weapons equipped by models in this unit have the **[SUSTAINED HITS 1]** ability.
*   **Kaptaris Stance:** Each time a melee attack targets this unit, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
*   **Rendax Stance:** Melee weapons equipped by models in this unit have the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability.

## Aegis of the Emperor
Models in **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** units (excluding **ANATHEMA PSYKANA** units) have a **4+ invulnerable save**. In addition, each time a model in such a unit would lose a wound as a result of a mortal wound, roll one D6: on a **4+**, that wound is not lost.

## Daughters of the Abyss
Models in **ANATHEMA PSYKANA** units have the **Feel No Pain 3+** ability against psychic attacks. In addition, enemy units subtract 1 from their **Battle-shock** tests for each **ANATHEMA PSYKANA** unit from your army that is within 6" of them.
