# Datasheet: Vigilators

Vigilators are the elite melee specialists of the Sisters of Silence, wielding massive executioner greatblades with a skill and strength that is truly terrifying. They are masters of the blade, able to bypass enemy defenses and deliver lethal strikes that can decapitate even the most powerful of foes. Their role is one of direct and brutal application of force, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are purged through the fire of their blades.
