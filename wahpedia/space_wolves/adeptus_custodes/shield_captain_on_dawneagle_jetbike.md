# Datasheet: Shield-Captain on Dawneagle Jetbike

Shield-Captains on Dawneagle jetbikes are the high-speed commanders of the Adeptus Custodes, leading their brothers in devastating aerial charges and lightning-fast strikes. Mounted atop these ornate combat platforms, they combine extreme mobility with overwhelming firepower and individual prowess. Their presence on the battlefield provides a significant boost to the tactical flexibility and defensive resilience of their mounted units, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are identified and eliminated with swift, calculated efficiency. Their role is one of both agile leadership and direct execution, guaranteeing that no foe can hide from the golden legion.
