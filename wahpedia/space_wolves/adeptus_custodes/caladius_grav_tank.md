# Datasheet: Caladius Grav-tank

The Caladius Grav-tank is a heavy armored platform that utilizes advanced repulsor technology to dominate the battlefield. Designed to provide overwhelming fire support for Adeptus Custodes ground forces, it is equipped with a variety of powerful weapon systems, from high-intensity accelerator cannons to devastating blaze cannons. Its agility and speed allow it to redeploy quickly to where its firepower is needed most, while its advanced defensive systems ensure it can weather significant punishment. Its role is one of armored dominance, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are eliminated before they can reach the Custodian lines.
