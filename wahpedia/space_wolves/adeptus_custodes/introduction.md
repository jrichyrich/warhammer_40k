# Introduction: Adeptus Custodes

The shining golden warriors known as the Adeptus Custodes are the elite bodyguard of the Emperor himself. Their word is law and their might in battle unsurpassed, even amongst the ranks of the Space Marines. Striding resplendent and fearless through the fog of war, they bring hope to the embattled civilisations of Mankind and swift death to their enemies.

They are the **Ten Thousand**, the ultimate defenders of the Golden Throne and the Imperial Palace on Terra. Each Custodian is a unique masterpiece of genetic engineering and tactical training, a warrior whose prowess is so great that they are said to be equal to a hundred lesser men. Clad in ornate auramite armor and wielding legendary guardian spears, they are the pinnacle of Humanity’s military might.

## Lore Overview
The Adeptus Custodes were created by the Emperor during the Unification Wars to be his personal guard and advisors. Since the end of the Horus Heresy, they have largely remained within the walls of the Imperial Palace, standing a ten-thousand-year vigil. However, with the return of Roboute Guilliman and the dawn of the Indomitus Era, the Custodes have once again marched forth from Terra to defend the Imperium across the galaxy.
