# Datasheet: Blade Champion

Blade Champions are among the most lethal duelists in the Adeptus Custodes, having dedicated their entire existence to the mastery of the sword. They are masters of a thousand combat forms, able to adapt their style to any opponent and strike with a speed and precision that is truly supernatural. Wielding their legendary Vaultswords, they are a whirlwind of blades on the battlefield, able to decapitate enemy leaders, sweep through hordes, or deliver crushing strikes against armored foes. Their role is one of direct and brutal application of skill, ensuring that no enemy can stand before them.

## Abilities

### **Faction**
*   **Martial Ka’tah**

### **Legacy of Combat**
While this model is leading a unit, that unit is eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced.

### **Consummate Swordsman**
Once per battle, at the start of the Fight phase, you can select one of the following:
*   Until the end of the phase, this model has the **Feel No Pain 4+** ability.
*   Until the end of the phase, each time this model makes a melee attack, a successful unmodified Hit roll of 5+ scores a **Critical Hit**.
