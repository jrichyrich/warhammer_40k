# Detachment: Talons of the Emperor

This detachment focuses on the tactical synergy and mutual protection between the Adeptus Custodes and the Anathema Psykana.

## Detachment Rule: Revered Companions
*   **ANATHEMA PSYKANA** units from your army gain the following ability:
    *   **Null Aegis (Aura):** While an **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** unit is within 6" of this unit, models in that unit have the Feel No Pain 5+ ability against Psychic Attacks and mortal wounds.
*   All other **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** units from your army gain the following ability:
    *   **Deadly Unity (Aura):** While an **ANATHEMA PSYKANA** unit is within 6" of this unit, each time a model in that **ANATHEMA PSYKANA** unit makes an attack, add 1 to the Hit roll.

## Enhancements
*   **Aegis Projector (20 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. Once per turn, the first time a saving throw is failed for the bearer’s unit, change the Damage characteristic of that attack to 0.
*   **Champion of the Imperium (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. The range of the bearer’s Null Aegis or Deadly Unity ability is increased to 9".
*   **Gift of Terran Artifice (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. Each time the bearer makes a melee attack, add 1 to the Wound roll.
*   **Radiant Mantle (30 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. Each time an attack targets the bearer’s unit, if the attacking model is within 12", subtract 1 from the Hit roll.

## Stratagems
*   **HUNT AS ONE (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* (Movement phase) Up to two units (one must be Anathema Psykana) can shoot and/or charge after Falling Back.
*   **TALONS INTERLOCKED (1CP):** *Battle Tactic.* (Shooting phase) Two units (one must be Anathema Psykana) gain +1 Strength and +1 AP against a single target.
*   **EMPYRIC SEVERANCE (1CP):** *Battle Tactic.* (Enemy Shooting or Fight phase) One unit near Anathema Psykana gains 4+ Feel No Pain against Psychic attacks/mortal wounds.
*   **EMPEROR’S EXECUTIONERS (2CP):** *Battle Tactic.* (Fight phase) Two units (one must be Anathema Psykana) get +1 to Wound against a unit below Starting Strength.
*   **TALONED PINCER (1CP):** *Battle Tactic.* (Enemy Movement phase) Two units (one must be Anathema Psykana) can make a 6" Normal move after an enemy ends a move within 9".
*   **SHIELD OF HONOUR (1CP):** *Epic Deed.* (Enemy Shooting phase) Attacks targeting an Anathema Psykana unit must instead target a nearby Adeptus Custodes infantry unit.
