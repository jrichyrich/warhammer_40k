# Datasheet: Custodian Guard with Adrasite and Pyrithite Spears

This specialized variant of the Custodian Guard is armed with rare and powerful weapons designed to eliminate the most formidable of targets. The adrasite spear unleashes devastating beams of energy that can disintegrate matter, while the pyrithite spear utilizes high-intensity melta fire to melt through the toughest armor. Their tactical role is one of specialized execution, providing the Custodes with the means to eliminate high-value enemy assets from afar before closing in for the final strike. Their presence in a mission is a guarantee of absolute destruction.
