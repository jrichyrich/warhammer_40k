# Datasheet: Witchseekers

Witchseekers are specialized Sisters of Silence tasked with purging psykers through the holy fire of their pyreblasters. Armed with these devastating flamethrowers, they are adept at flushing enemies out of cover and disrupting their formations. Their role is one of both psychological and physical disruption, using the intense heat and the threat of immolation to break the will of their prey. Their specialized training and equipment make them a terrifying presence on the battlefield, especially for those who rely on the warp for their power.
