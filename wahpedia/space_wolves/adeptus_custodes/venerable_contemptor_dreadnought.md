# Datasheet: Venerable Contemptor Dreadnought

Venerable Contemptor Dreadnoughts are ancient combat walkers that house the fallen heroes of the Adeptus Custodes. Utilizing advanced systems and a superior auramite sarcophagus, they are nearly indestructible anchors on the battlefield, combining extreme durability with devastating weaponry. Their role is one of direct and brutal application of force, ensuring that no enemy can stand before the Emperor's justice.
