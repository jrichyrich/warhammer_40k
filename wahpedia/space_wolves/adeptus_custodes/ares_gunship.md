# Datasheet: Ares Gunship

The Ares Gunship is a heavy aerial combat platform of the Adeptus Custodes, designed to provide devastating close air support and air-to-ground dominance. Equipped with a massive arachnus heavy cannon and multiple infernus bolt cannons, it is a mobile fortress in the sky, able to vaporize the most formidable of enemy armor and monsters. Its agility and speed allow it to redeploy quickly to where its overwhelming firepower is needed most, while its advanced defensive systems ensure it can weather significant punishment. Its role is one of direct and brutal air superiority, ensuring that no enemy can hide from the Emperor's judgment.

### **Infernus Firebomb**
Each time this model ends a **Normal move**, you can select one enemy unit it moved over. Until the end of the turn, that unit cannot have the **Benefit of Cover**. Additionally, roll one D6 for each model in that unit; for every 6, the unit suffers 1 mortal wound.

### **Damaged: 1-7 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-7 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
