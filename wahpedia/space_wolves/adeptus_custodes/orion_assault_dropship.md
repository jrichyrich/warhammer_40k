# Datasheet: Orion Assault Dropship

The Orion Assault Dropship is a massive heavy transport and aerial combat platform of the Adeptus Custodes, designed to deliver full squads of the Ten Thousand directly into the heart of the enemy's lines. Heavily armed and armored, it is able to weather intense fire while providing overwhelming fire support from its multiple arachnus heavy cannons and heavy bolt launchers. Its agility and speed allow it to redeploy quickly to where its massive transport capacity and devastating firepower are needed most. Its role is one of armored delivery and aerial dominance, ensuring that the Emperor's will is maintained against any odds.
