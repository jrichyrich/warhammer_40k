# Datasheet: Contemptor-Galatus Dreadnought

The Contemptor-Galatus Dreadnought is a defensive powerhouse of the Adeptus Custodes, designed to weather the most intense enemy fire and hold vital positions. Housing a fallen hero of the Ten Thousand and equipped with a massive Galatus shield and a powerful warblade, it is nearly immovable on the battlefield. Its role is one of steadfast defense and counter-attack, using its supernatural resilience to anchor the Custodian lines and ensure the Emperor's will is maintained against any odds.
