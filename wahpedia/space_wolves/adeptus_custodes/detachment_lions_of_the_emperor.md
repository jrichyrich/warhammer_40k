# Detachment: Lions of the Emperor

This detachment highlights the overwhelming power and individual resilience of the Golden Legion, especially when operating in small, elite groups.

## Detachment Rule: Against All Odds
Each time a model in an **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** unit (excluding **VEHICLES**) makes an attack, if there are no other friendly units within 6" of that unit, add 1 to the Hit roll and add 1 to the Wound roll.

## Enhancements
*   **Superior Creation (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES INFANTRY** only. Resurrect on a 2+ with full wounds remaining.
*   **Praesidius (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. The bearer has **Lone Operative** and **Stealth**.
*   **Fierce Conqueror (15 pts):** **SHIELD-CAPTAIN** only. Add +2 Attacks for every 5 enemy models within 6".
*   **Admonimortis (10 pts):** **SHIELD-CAPTAIN** only. Improve melee weapons by +3 Strength, and +1 AP/Damage.

## Stratagems
*   **GILDED CHAMPION (1CP):** (Any phase) **CHARACTER** can use a "once per battle" ability a second time.
*   **DEFIANT TO THE LAST (1CP):** (Fight phase) Destroyed model can fight before removal on a 4+ (+2 for **CHARACTERS**).
*   **PEERLESS WARRIOR (1CP):** (Fight phase) Melee weapons gain **[PRECISION]**.
*   **UNLEASH THE LIONS (1CP):** (Command phase) Split an **ALLARUS** or **AQUILON** unit into individual models.
*   **MANOEUVRE AND FIRE (1CP):** (Movement phase) Unit can shoot and charge after Falling Back.
*   **SWIFT AS THE EAGLE (1CP):** (Enemy Shooting phase) Unit can make a Normal move of up to D6" after being shot.
