# Datasheet: Aleya

Aleya, a Knight-Centura of the Sisters of Silence, is a formidable warrior and a master of the anti-psychic arts. Her presence on the battlefield is a bane to any psyker, her sheer will and specialized training allowing her to disrupt their powers and strike them down with cold, calculated precision. Wielding the legendary sword Somnus, she is a lethal duelist, able to bypass enemy defenses and strike at the heart of their command. Her role is one of both hunter and protector, ensuring the Emperor's enemies are purged and his servants are shielded from the corruption of the warp.
