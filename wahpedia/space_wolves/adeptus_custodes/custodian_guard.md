# Datasheet: Custodian Guard

Custodian Guard are the foundational warriors of the Adeptus Custodes, each one a masterpiece of genetic engineering and tactical training. Clad in ornate auramite armor and wielding legendary guardian spears or sentinel blades, they are nearly indestructible anchors on the battlefield. Their tactical flexibility and hard-won experience allow them to adapt to any mission, from securing vital objectives to eliminating the most powerful of threats.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deep Strike**

### **Faction**
*   **Martial Ka’tah**

### **Guardian**
Each time a model in this unit makes an attack, re-roll a Wound roll of 1. While this unit is within range of an objective marker you control, you can re-roll the Wound roll instead.

### **Sentinel Storm**
Once per battle, in your Shooting phase, after this unit has shot, it can shoot again.

## Wargear Abilities
### **Praesidium Shield**
The bearer's Wounds characteristic is 4 instead of 3.
