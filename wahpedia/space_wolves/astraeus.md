# Datasheet: Astraeus

The Astraeus Super-heavy Tank is a massive Primaris combat platform, a mobile fortress designed to dominate the battlefield. Utilizing advanced repulsor technology, it can hover over obstacles while unleashing a devastating hail of fire from its twin macro-accelerator cannons and an array of auxiliary weapons. It is a symbol of the Imperium's renewed military might, able to suppress entire enemy formations through sheer weight of fire.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D6+2**
*   **Hover**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Suppression Fire**
In your Shooting phase, after this model has shot, select one enemy unit hit by one or more attacks made with its **twin macro-accelerator cannon** this phase. Until the start of your next turn, that enemy unit is suppressed. While a unit is suppressed, each time a model in that unit makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.

### **Invulnerable Save**
This model has a 5+ invulnerable save against ranged attacks.

### **Damaged: 1-8 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-8 wounds remaining, subtract 4 from its Objective Control characteristic and each time this model makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
