# Datasheet: Rhino

The Rhino is the standard armored personnel carrier of the Space Marines, a reliable and versatile transport designed to deliver squads of infantry safely to the front lines. Its rugged construction and ease of maintenance make it an essential asset for any mission, providing a mobile base of operations and protecting its passengers from small-arms fire. Its ability to self-repair and its versatile firing deck make it a reliable and effective transport on any battlefield.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deadly Demise D3**

### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Self Repair**
At the end of your Command phase, this model regains 1 lost wound.

### **Firing Deck 2**
In your Shooting phase, you can select up to 2 models embarked in this transport. For each model selected, you can select one ranged weapon equipped by that model; this transport model is treated as being equipped with those weapons until the end of the phase.
