# Detachment: Stormlance Task Force

The Stormlance Task Force is a formation that emphasizes speed and rapid movement, using highly mobile units to outmanoeuvre and strike at the enemy from unexpected directions.

## Detachment Rule: Lightning Assault
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units from your army are eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which they Advanced or Fell Back.

## Enhancements
*   **Fury of the Storm (25 pts):** **MOUNTED** model only. Add 1 to the Strength and Armour Penetration characteristics of the bearer's melee weapons. If the bearer made a Charge move this turn, add 2 instead.
*   **Portents of Wisdom (15 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, each time you make an Advance roll for that unit, you can re-roll that roll.
*   **Feinting Withdrawal (10 pts):** **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, that unit is eligible to shoot in a turn in which it Fell Back.
*   **Hunter’s Instincts (25 pts):** **MOUNTED** model only. While the bearer is in Strategic Reserves, for the purpose of setting up the bearer's unit on the battlefield, treat the current battle round number as being one higher than it actually is.

## Stratagems
*   **Armour of Contempt (1CP):** (Incoming attack) Worsen the AP characteristic of that attack by 1.
*   **Blitzing Fusillade (1CP):** (Shooting phase) Ranged weapons equipped by models in one unit gain the [ASSAULT] ability (or [SUSTAINED HITS 1] if they already have it).
*   **Full Throttle (2CP):** (Movement phase) One unit can auto-advance 6" (or 9" if it has the **MOUNTED** keyword).
*   **Shock Assault (1CP):** (Charge phase) One **MOUNTED** unit can re-roll its Charge roll and its melee weapons gain the [LANCE] ability.
*   **Ride Hard, Ride Fast (1CP):** (Enemy Shooting phase) One **MOUNTED** or **FLY** unit subtracts 1 from the Hit and Wound rolls of incoming attacks.
*   **Wind-swift Evasion (1CP):** (Enemy move) When an enemy unit ends a move within 9", your unit can move up to 6".
