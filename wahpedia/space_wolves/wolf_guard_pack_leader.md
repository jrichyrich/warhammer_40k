# Datasheet: Wolf Guard Pack Leader

Wolf Guard Pack Leaders are veteran warriors who are assigned to lead and inspire their younger brothers in the heat of battle. They are highly respected for their experience and their skill in combat, and their presence in a pack often provides a significant boost to its morale and effectiveness.

## Abilities
### **Faction**
*   **Oath of Moment**

### **Pack Leader**
This model cannot be your **WARLORD** and cannot be given Enhancements.

### **Inspiring Leader**
While this model is leading a unit, once per battle, when a Battle-shock test is taken for that unit, you can re-roll that test.

## Wargear Options
*   This model can have its boltgun replaced with one of the following: 1 plasma pistol, 1 power weapon, 1 power fist, 1 thunder hammer, 1 storm shield.
