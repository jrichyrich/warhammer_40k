# FAQ: Space Marines & Space Wolves

This FAQ covers common clarifications for Space Marines and Space Wolves as of the 10th Edition Faction Pack and Codex updates.

## Core Rules & Stratagems
*   **Detachments:** Every Detachment in *Codex: Space Marines* and the *Space Marines Faction Pack* is considered a **Codex: Space Marines** Detachment.
*   **Gladius Task Force:** A Combat Doctrine does **not** need to be active to use the *Adaptive Strategy* Stratagem.
*   **Hellblasters:** If destroyed by anything other than an attack or Hazardous test, they **cannot** use the *For the Chapter!* ability.
*   **Captains:** The *Rites of Battle* ability **can** target the Captain’s unit while it is in Reserves or Strategic Reserves.
*   **Objective Markers:** A marker sabotaged via *A Deadly Prize* remains sabotaged even if the opponent controls it, though its mortal wound ability is inactive while they hold it.

## Transports & Firing Deck
*   **Firing Deck:** Weapons fired via a Transport's *Firing Deck* do **not** benefit from Leader abilities (e.g., *Lethal Hits* from a Lieutenant) because the Transport is not part of the Attached unit.
*   **Impulsors:** *Orbital Comms Array* abilities are Auras and do **not** stack. You cannot roll for a second Impulsor if the first one fails to grant a CP for the same Stratagem use.

## Oath of Moment
*   If an Attached unit ceases to be attached, the effect persists on the remaining unit.
*   Units embarked within a Transport **cannot** be selected as the target.
*   Units in Reserves **can** be selected as the target.

## Enhancements & Special Rules
*   **Enhancements:** Units in Reserves **can** use Enhancements (following all restrictions).
*   **Astartes Banner:** Multiple instances (e.g., from an Ancient) **do** stack.
*   **Stormspeeder Thunderstrike:** The +1 to Wound roll only applies **after** all weapons from the model have finished shooting; it does not apply to subsequent weapons from the same model in the same phase.
*   **Rapid Ingress:**
    *   **Cannot** be used on a Drop Pod in the first battle round if mission rules (like Pariah Nexus) prohibit round 1 arrivals.
    *   **Can** be used in the first battle round for units with the *Hunter’s Instincts* Enhancement.
*   **Roboute Guilliman:** If splitting attacks between two *Oath of Moment* targets, the second target's re-rolls only become active **after** all attacks from the unit are resolved.
