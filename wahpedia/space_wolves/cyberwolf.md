# Datasheet: Cyberwolf

Cyberwolves are Fenrisian Wolves that have been enhanced with cybernetic implants and combat sub-routines. They are used as trackers, hunters, and scouts, their enhanced senses and physical capabilities making them exceptionally effective in these roles. They often lead packs of their organic kin into battle, coordinating their attacks with animalistic precision.
