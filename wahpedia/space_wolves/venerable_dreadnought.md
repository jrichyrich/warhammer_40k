# Datasheet: Space Wolves Venerable Dreadnought

The Venerable Dreadnoughts of the Space Wolves are among the oldest and most respected warriors of the Chapter. Often interred for millennia, they are living repositories of Fenrisian history and sagas. In battle, they are nearly indestructible anchors, combining ancient wisdom with devastating weaponry and the iconic Blizzard Shield.
