# Datasheet: Razorback

The Razorback is a versatile infantry support vehicle built on the Rhino chassis, sacrificing transport capacity for a powerful turret-mounted weapon system. Designed to provide mobile fire support for the squads it carries, it can be equipped with heavy bolters or lascannons to eliminate a variety of threats. Its advanced targeting systems and ability to coordinate fire with disembarked troops make it a reliable and effective asset on any battlefield.

### **Fire Support**
In your Shooting phase, after this model has shot, select one enemy unit hit by one or more attacks made by this model's turret weapon this phase. Until the end of the phase, each time a friendly model that disembarked from this transport this turn makes an attack that targets that enemy unit, you can re-roll the Wound roll.
