# Datasheet: Stormwolf

The Stormwolf is a specialized assault transport, designed to deliver Space Wolves units directly into the heart of the enemy's lines. It is heavily armed and armored, able to weather intense fire while its passengers prepare to leap into the fray. Its agility and speed make it a terrifying sight on the battlefield, a harbinger of the lupine fury about to be unleashed.

## Abilities
### **Into the Foe**
If a unit disembarks from this transport before it has moved, that unit is eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced.

### **Hover**
In your Command phase, this model can hover. If it does, until the start of your next Command phase, its Move characteristic becomes 20" and it loses the **AIRCRAFT** keyword and the associated rules for movement and attacks.

### **Transport**
This model has a transport capacity of 16 **ADEPTUS ASTARTES INFANTRY** models. Each **JUMP PACK**, **WULFEN**, **GRAVIS**, or **TERMINATOR** model takes up 2 spaces, and each **CENTURION** model takes up 3 spaces.

## Unit Composition
