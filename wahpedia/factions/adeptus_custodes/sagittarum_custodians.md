# Datasheet: Sagittarum Custodians

Sagittarum Custodians are specialized fire-support infantry of the Adeptus Custodes, armed with powerful bolt calivers that can unleash a devastating hail of fire or high-intensity disintegration blasts. They are masters of long-range engagement, able to suppress enemy formations and eliminate high-value targets from afar. Their tactical role is one of consistent and lethal ranged support, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are eliminated before they can reach the Custodian lines. Their presence in a mission is a guarantee of superior firepower and tactical dominance.

### **Golden Volley**
Once per battle, in your Shooting phase, after this unit has shot, select one enemy unit hit by one or more of those attacks. That enemy unit must take a Battle-shock test.
