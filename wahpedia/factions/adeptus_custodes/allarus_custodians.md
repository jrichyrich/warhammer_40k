# Datasheet: Allarus Custodians

Allarus Custodians are the heavy shock infantry of the Adeptus Custodes, clad in massive suits of Allarus Terminator armor and armed with devastating guardian spears or castellan axes. They are masters of the teleport strike, appearing suddenly in the heart of the enemy's lines to unleash a whirlwind of destruction. Their tactical role is one of decapitation and disruption, using their extreme durability and overwhelming firepower to eliminate high-value targets and scatter enemy formations.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deep Strike**

### **Faction**
*   **Martial Ka’tah**

### **Slayers of Tyrants**
Each time a model in this unit makes an attack that targets a **CHARACTER**, **MONSTER**, or **VEHICLE** unit, you can re-roll the Wound roll.

### **From Golden Light**
Once per battle, at the end of your opponent’s Reinforcements step, if this unit is not within Engagement Range of any enemy units, you can remove it from the battlefield and place it into Strategic Reserves.
