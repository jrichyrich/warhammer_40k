# Datasheet: Agamatus Custodians

Agamatus Custodians are the heavy-mounted elite of the Adeptus Custodes, riding to battle atop massive Gyrfalcon jetbikes. They are the Chapter's primary armored response force, utilizing their extreme speed and heavy weaponry to engage and destroy the most formidable of targets. Equipped with a variety of powerful weapon systems, from high-intensity adrathic devastators to reality-warping mag-cannons, they are able to dominate the battlefield and eliminate any threat to the Imperium. Their role is one of armored disruption and execution, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are identifiable only by the craters they leave behind.
