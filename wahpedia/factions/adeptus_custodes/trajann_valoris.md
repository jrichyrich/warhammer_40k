# Datasheet: Trajann Valoris

Trajann Valoris, the Captain-General of the Adeptus Custodes, is one of the most powerful warriors in the Imperium. A master of tactical warfare and a peerless duelist, he leads the Ten Thousand with a wisdom and strength that few can match. Armed with the legendary Watcher’s Axe and protected by the most advanced auramite armor, he is a living symbol of the Emperor's will, able to turn the tide of any battle through his sheer presence and skill.

### **Captain-General**
While this model is leading a unit, you can ignore any or all modifiers to the characteristics of models in that unit and to any or all modifiers to rolls or tests made for models in that unit (excluding saving throws).

### **Moment Shackle**
Once per battle, at the start of the Fight phase, you can select one of the following:
*   Until the end of the phase, this model has the **Feel No Pain 2+** ability.
*   Until the end of the phase, add 2 to the Attacks characteristic of this model's melee weapons.
*   Until the end of the phase, this model is eligible to fight a second time.
