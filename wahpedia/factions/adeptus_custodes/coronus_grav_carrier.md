# Datasheet: Coronus Grav-carrier

The Coronus Grav-carrier is a heavy assault transport that utilizes advanced repulsor technology to deliver Adeptus Custodes ground forces directly into the heart of the enemy's lines. Designed to weather intense fire while its passengers prepare to leap into the fray, it is equipped with a variety of powerful weapon systems to provide overwhelming fire support. Its agility and speed make it a terrifying sight on the battlefield, a harbinger of the golden fury about to be unleashed.
