# Datasheet: Telemon Heavy Dreadnought

The Telemon Heavy Dreadnought is the ultimate combat walker of the Adeptus Custodes, a massive engine of destruction that combines incredible durability with overwhelming firepower. Housing the most ancient and honored fallen heroes of the Ten Thousand, it is a living symbol of the Chapter's long vigil and its commitment to the defense of the Imperial Palace. Equipped with an array of devastating weapon systems, from high-intensity accelerator culverins to reality-warping blaze cannons, it is able to dominate the battlefield and eliminate any threat to the Imperium. Its presence in a mission is a guarantee of the Emperor's final judgment.
