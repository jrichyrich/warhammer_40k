# Datasheet: Venerable Land Raider

The Venerable Land Raider is the primary heavy battle transport of the Adeptus Custodes, a nearly indestructible mobile fortress that combines massive armor with overwhelming firepower. Utilizing advanced sensors and a front-mounted assault ramp, it can deliver its passengers directly into the heart of the enemy's lines, allowing them to charge immediately after disembarking. It is a symbol of the Chapter's unstoppable might, ensuring that the Emperor's will is carried out through the direct and brutal application of force.

### **Assault Ramp**
Each time a unit disembarks from this transport after it has made a Normal move, that unit is still eligible to declare a charge this turn.

### **Damaged: 1-5 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-5 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
