# Adeptus Custodes Detachments

The Adeptus Custodes have access to specialized tactical formations, each representing a different aspect of their role as the Emperor's bodyguards and executors.

## Unique Adeptus Custodes Detachments
*   [Talons of the Emperor](detachment_talons_of_the_emperor.md) - Focuses on the synergy between the Custodes and the Sisters of Silence.
*   [Shield Host](detachment_shield_host.md) - Emphasizes the defensive and counter-attacking prowess of the Custodes.
*   [Null Maiden Vigil](detachment_null_maiden_vigil.md) - Specializes in the anti-psychic and disruption tactics of the Anathema Psykana.
*   [Auric Champions](detachment_auric_champions.md) - Focuses on the heroic deeds and leadership of individual Custodian champions.
*   [Solar Spearhead](detachment_solar_spearhead.md) - Emphasizes high-speed movement and shock assaults.
*   [Lions of the Emperor](detachment_lions_of_the_emperor.md) - Highlights the overwhelming power and resilience of the Golden Legion.

## Boarding Action Detachments
*   [Voyagers in Darkness](detachment_voyagers_in_darkness.md) - Specialized rules for ship-to-ship boarding actions.
*   [Black Ship Guardians](detachment_black_ship_guardians.md) - Rules for defending the mysterious Black Ships of the Adeptus Astra Telepathica.
