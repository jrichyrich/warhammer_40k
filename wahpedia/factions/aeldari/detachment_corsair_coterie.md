# Detachment: Corsair Coterie

## Detachment Rule: Relentless Raiders
*   Enemies ending move on your objective take D3 mortal wounds on a 2+.
*   **ANHRATHE** gain **Void Thieves** (Objective markers remain under control).

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pirates’ Due** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Re-roll Wound rolls of 1 (full re-roll for ANHRATHE on objective). |
| **Lethal Ruse** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Charge after Falling Back; ANHRATHE cause mortal wounds. |
| **Outcast Ambush** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | RANGERS/SHROUD RUNNERS gain **[Ignores Cover], [Rapid Fire 1]**, +1 AP. |
| **Into the Breach** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | ANHRATHE make D6+1" move after destroying a unit. |
| **Cloak and Shadow** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | INFANTRY on objective gain Stealth and 18" target limit. |
| **Vengeful Sorrow** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Surge move after models are destroyed. |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Infamy (Aura) (25 pts):** *ANHRATHE only.* -1 OC to enemies within 3".
*   **Webway Pathstone (25 pts):** *ANHRATHE only.* Deep Strike; can return to Strategic Reserves.
*   **Archraider (35 pts):** *ANHRATHE CHARACTER only.* Increase opponent's Stratagem cost by 1CP.
*   **Voidstone (15 pts):** *ANHRATHE INFANTRY only.* Unit gains 5+ invulnerable save.
