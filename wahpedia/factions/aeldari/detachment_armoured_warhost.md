# Detachment: Armoured Warhost

## Detachment Rule: Skilled Crews
*   **VEHICLE** ranged weapons gain **[ASSAULT]**.
*   Re-roll Advance rolls for **FLY VEHICLES**.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Layered Wards** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | VEHICLE gains FNP 5+ against mortal wounds. |
| **Swift Deployment** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Disembark after Advancing. |
| **Vectored Engines** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | FLY VEHICLE eligible to shoot after Falling Back. |
| **Cloudstrike** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | VEHICLE gains Deep Strike (can set up >6" away). |
| **Soulsight** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Re-roll one Hit, Wound, and Damage roll for VEHICLE. |
| **Anti-Grav Repulsion** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | -2 to Charge rolls against FLY VEHICLE. |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Guiding Presence (25 pts):** *PSYKER only.* +1 to Hit for VEHICLE within 9".
*   **Harmonisation Matrix (30 pts):** *AELDARI only.* On a 3+, gain 1CP if on an objective.
*   **Spirit Stone of Raelyth (20 pts):** *PSYKER only.* Lone Operative near VEHICLE; heal VEHICLE D3 wounds.
*   **Guileful Strategist (15 pts):** *AELDARI only.* Redeploy three VEHICLE units.
