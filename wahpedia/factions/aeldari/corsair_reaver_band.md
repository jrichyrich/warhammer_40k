### Abilities
**Reaver Band:** Once per turn, when an enemy unit ends a Normal, Advance or Fall Back move within 9" of this unit, if this unit is not within Engagement Range of one or more enemy units, it can make a Normal move of up to D6".

### Unit Composition & Wargear
*   **0-1 Corsair Reaver Felarch**
*   **5-10 Corsair Reavers**
*   **Equipped with:** Brace of pistols; Corsair firearm; close combat weapon.
*   **Options:**
    *   Any number of models can replace their Corsair firearm with 1 spar-glaive.
    *   For every 5 models, 1 model can replace its Corsair firearm with: 1 Aeldari missile launcher, 1 blaster, 1 dark lance, 1 flamer, 1 fusion gun, 1 shredder, 1 shuriken cannon, or 1 splinter cannon.
    *   The Felarch can also be equipped with: 1 dissonance pistol or 1 void sabre.
