### Abilities
- **Core**: Leader
- **Faction**: Battle Focus
- **Aspect Training**: While leading **Howling Banshees**, gains Fights First. While leading **Striking Scorpions**, gains Infiltrators, Scouts 7", and Stealth.
- **Superlative Strategist**: While leading a unit, you can re-roll Advance rolls for that unit.
- **Path of Command**: Once per battle round, one model with this ability can reduce the CP cost of a Stratagem targeting its unit by 1CP.
