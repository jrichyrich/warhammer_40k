# Autarch Wayleaper
- **M**: 14" | **T**: 3 | **Sv**: 3+ | **W**: 4 | **Ld**: 6+ | **OC**: 1
- **Invulnerable Save**: 4+

### Ranged & Melee Weapons
*(Same options as the standard Autarch)*

### Abilities
- **Core**: Deep Strike, Leader
- **Faction**: Battle Focus
- **Indomitable Strength of Will**: While leading a unit, each time you spend a Battle Focus token for an Agile Manoeuvre, on a 3+ you gain 1 Battle Focus token.
- **Path of Command**: Once per battle round, reduce CP cost of a Stratagem targeting its unit by 1CP.

- **Composition**: 1 Autarch Wayleaper (80 pts)
- **Equipped with**: Shuriken pistol; star glaive.
- **Leader**: Can be attached to: **Swooping Hawks**, **Warp Spiders**.
- **Keywords**: Infantry, Character, Aeldari, Jump Pack, Fly, Grenades, Autarch Wayleaper.
