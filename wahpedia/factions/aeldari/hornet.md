# Datasheet: Hornet

The Hornet is a fast, light attack craft designed for hit-and-run harassment, utilizing its extreme speed and maneuverability to strike at the enemy's weak points. Equipped with a variety of powerful weapon systems, it can engage a range of targets, while its lightning-fast movement allows it to perform devastating fly-over attacks that can disrupt and damage enemy formations. Its role is one of agile and aggressive support, often acting as a scout or a harrier for larger Aeldari warhosts.
