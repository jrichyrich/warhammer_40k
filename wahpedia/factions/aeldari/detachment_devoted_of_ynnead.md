# Detachment: Devoted of Ynnead

## Detachment Rule: Strength from Death
*   **Lethal Intent:** 6" move after a unit is destroyed.
*   **Lethal Surge:** Enhanced Fade Back move.
*   **Lethal Reprisal:** Fights First if below Starting Strength.
*   **ASURYANI** gain the **YNNARI** keyword. Must include **YVRAINE** or **THE YNCARNE** as Warlord.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pall of Dread** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Sticky objective after unit is destroyed. |
| **Macabre Resilience** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | -1 to Wound roll against target unit. |
| **Emissaries of Ynnead** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Re-roll Hit rolls of 1 (full re-roll if below Starting Strength). |
| **Parting the Veil** | 2CP | Strategic Ploy | Fight on death (guaranteed). |
| **Soulsight** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Ranged weapons gain **[Lethal Hits]** and **[Ignores Cover]**. |
| **Death Answers Death** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Shoot back after models are destroyed in Shooting phase. |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Gaze of Ynnead (15 pts):** *FARSEER only.* Eldritch Storm gains **[Devastating Wounds]**.
*   **Storm of Whispers (10 pts):** *WARLOCK only.* Target hit by attacks must take Battle-shock test.
*   **Borrowed Vigour (10 pts):** *ARCHON only.* +2 Attacks to melee weapons.
*   **Morbid Might (15 pts):** *SUCCUBUS only.* Re-roll Wound rolls in melee.
