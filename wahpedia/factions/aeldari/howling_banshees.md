#### **Abilities**
**Core:** Fights First
**Faction:** Strands of Fate

*   **Acrobatic:** This unit is eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which it Advanced or Fell Back.
*   **Invulnerable Save:** 5+ (Improved to 4+ against melee attacks).

#### **Wargear Abilities**
*   **Aspect Shrine Token:** Once per battle for each Aspect Shrine token this unit has, you can change the result of one Hit roll or one Wound roll made for a model in this unit (excluding CHARACTER models) to an unmodified 6.

---

#### **Unit Composition**
*   1 Howling Banshee Exarch
*   4-9 Howling Banshees
*   **Equipped with:** Shuriken pistol, Banshee blade.

#### **Wargear Options**
*   The Exarch's Banshee blade can be replaced with 1 Executioner or 1 Triskele.
*   The Exarch's Shuriken pistol and Banshee blade can be replaced with Mirrorswords.
*   For every 5 models in this unit, it can have 1 Aspect Shrine token.
