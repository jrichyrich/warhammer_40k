# Detachment: Warhost

## Detachment Rule: Martial Grace
*   Receive **1 additional Battle Focus token** at the start of the battle round.
*   **Swift as the Wind Agile Manoeuvre:** Add an additional 1" to Move characteristic (total +3").
*   **Agile Manoeuvres:** Each time a unit performs an Agile Manoeuvre that involves rolling a D6, add 1 to the result.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | When / Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lightning-Fast Reactions** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | **Opponent's Shooting/Fight phase.** Subtract 1 from Hit rolls against one ASURYANI unit (excluding Wraith Constructs). |
| **Skyborne Sanctuary** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | **End of Fight phase.** An ASURYANI unit within 6" of a TRANSPORT can embark within it if not in Engagement Range. |
| **Feigned Retreat** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | **Movement phase.** An ASURYANI unit that Fell Back is eligible to shoot and charge this turn. |
| **Blitzing Firepower** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | **Shooting phase.** ASURYANI unit gains [SUSTAINED HITS 1] against targets within 12". |
| **Fire and Fade** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | **Shooting phase.** After an ASURYANI INFANTRY unit (non-Wraith/Aircraft) shoots, it can make a Normal move of D6+1". |
| **Webway Tunnel** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | **End of opponent's Fight phase.** An ASURYANI INFANTRY unit within 9" of a board edge is placed into Strategic Reserves. |

## Enhancements
*   **Phoenix Gem (35 pts):** *ASURYANI model only.* The first time the bearer is destroyed, on a 2+, set them back up with full wounds.
*   **Timeless Strategist (15 pts):** *ASURYANI model only.* If the bearer is on the battlefield, receive 1 additional Battle Focus token at the start of the battle round.
*   **Gift of Foresight (15 pts):** *ASURYANI model only.* Once per battle round, target the bearer's unit with the *Command Re-roll* Stratagem for 0CP.
*   **Psychic Destroyer (30 pts):** *ASURYANI PSYKER model only.* Add 1 to the Damage characteristic of the bearer's ranged Psychic weapons.
