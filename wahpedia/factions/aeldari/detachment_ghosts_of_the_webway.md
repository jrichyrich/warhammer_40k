# Detachment: Ghosts of the Webway

## Detachment Rule: Acrobatic Onslaught
*   **HARLEQUINS** can move through enemy models on a Charge.
*   **TROUPE** units gain **BATTLELINE** and OC 2.
*   Limit 3 of: DEATH JESTER, SHADOWSEER, TROUPE MASTER.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Staged Death** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Set destroyed CHARACTER back up with half wounds at end of phase. |
| **Heroes’ Fall** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Fight on death for HARLEQUINS on a 4+. |
| **Mocking Flight** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Eligible to shoot and charge after Falling Back. |
| **Tricksters’ Retort** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | 6" Normal move when enemy ends move within 9". |
| **Bloody Dance** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Declare a charge in the opponent's Charge phase. |
| **Exit the Stage** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Remove unit to Strategic Reserves at end of opponent's Fight phase. |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Cegorach’s Coil (25 pts):** *TROUPE MASTER only.* Mortal wounds on the charge (4+ per model).
*   **Mask of Secrets (15 pts):** *HARLEQUINS only.* Forced Desperate Escape tests for enemies Falling Back.
*   **Murder’s Jest (20 pts):** *DEATH JESTER only.* Critical Hits on successful hits against Below Half-strength units.
*   **Mistweave (15 pts):** *SHADOWSEER only.* Leading unit gains **Infiltrators**.
