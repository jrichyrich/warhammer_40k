# Detachment: Eldritch Raiders

## Detachment Rule: Yriel’s Own
*   **AELDARI** units can charge after Advancing.
*   **ANHRATHE, RANGERS, SHROUD RUNNERS** re-roll Advance rolls.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Raiders’ Spoils** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | +1 OC to ANHRATHE unit in Engagement Range. |
| **Ruthless Killers** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | +1 Damage to CORSAIR VOIDSCARRED attacks. |
| **Yriel’s Example** | 1CP | Epic Deed | INFANTRY unit gains FNP 5+. |
| **No Prey Too Big** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | +1 to Wound if Strength < Toughness. |
