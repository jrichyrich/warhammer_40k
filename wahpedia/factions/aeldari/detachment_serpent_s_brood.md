# Detachment: Serpent’s Brood

## Detachment Rule: Boons of the Brood
*   **HARLEQUIN MOUNTED/VEHICLES** gain **[Sustained Hits 1]**.
*   Units gain **[Sustained Hits 1]** after disembarking.
*   **TROUPE** units gain **BATTLELINE** and OC 2.

## Enhancements
*   **Key of Ghosts (20 pts):** *HARLEQUINS only.* Unit gains **Scouts 6"**.
*   **Weavers’ Wail (20 pts):** *TROUPE MASTER only.* +3 Strength and +1 Attacks in melee.
*   **Fanged Leer (10 pts):** *DEATH JESTER only.* Select two abilities for shrieker cannon.
*   **Shedskin Raiment (25 pts):** *SHADOWSEER only.* Redeploy three HARLEQUINS units.
