**ABILITIES**
*   **Faction:** Battle Focus, Disparate Paths
*   **Dance of Death:** At the start of the Fight phase, select one:
    *   **Hero's Prowess:** Re-roll Hit rolls of 1.
    *   **Villain's Doom:** +1 to Wound rolls.
    *   **Trickster's Grace:** -1 to be Hit.
*   **Flip Belt:** Ignore vertical distance when moving.

**WARGEAR OPTIONS**
*   Any number of Harlequin's blades can be replaced with Harlequin's special weapons.
*   Lead Player's Harlequin's blade can be replaced with 1 power sword.
*   Shuriken pistols can be replaced with Neuro disruptors or Fusion pistols (Max 2 each if unit < 10 models; Max 4 each if unit 10+).

**UNIT COMPOSITION**
