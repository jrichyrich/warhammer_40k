# Dark Angels Detachments

The Dark Angels have several specialized detachments in the 10th Edition of Warhammer 40,000, reflecting their unique company organizations and combat styles.

## Available Detachments

1. [Unforgiven Task Force](detachment_unforgiven_task_force.md)
   - *Core Theme:* Resilience and "Grim Resolve."
2. [Inner Circle Task Force](detachment_inner_circle_task_force.md)
   - *Core Theme:* Elite **DEATHWING** and **INFANTRY** focused attacks.
3. [Company of Hunters](detachment_company_of_hunters.md)
   - *Core Theme:* High-speed **RAVENWING** and **MOUNTED** reconnaissance.

Each detachment provides a unique **Detachment Rule**, six **Stratagems**, and four **Enhancements** to further specialize your Dark Angels army.
