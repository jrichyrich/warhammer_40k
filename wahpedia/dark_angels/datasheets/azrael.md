## Abilities
**CORE:** **[LEADER]**

**Supreme Grand Master:** While this model is leading a unit, weapons equipped by models in that unit have the **[SUSTAINED HITS 1]** ability.

**Masterful Tactician:** At the start of your Command phase, if this model is on the battlefield, you gain 1 CP.

**The Lion Helm:** While this model is leading a unit, models in that unit have a 4+ invulnerable save.

**Watcher in the Dark:** Once per battle, in any phase, this model can summon a Watcher in the Dark. Until the end of that phase, models in the bearer’s unit have the **[FEEL NO PAIN 4+]** ability against mortal wounds.
