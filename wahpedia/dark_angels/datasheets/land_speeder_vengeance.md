## Abilities
**CORE:** **[DEADLY DEMISE D3]**, **[DEEP STRIKE]**

**Storm of Vengeance:** Once per turn, in your opponent’s Shooting phase, when a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit within 6" of this model is destroyed (excluding this model itself), this model can shoot as if it were your Shooting phase. It can only target the enemy unit that just destroyed that friendly unit, and can only do so if that enemy unit is an eligible target.
