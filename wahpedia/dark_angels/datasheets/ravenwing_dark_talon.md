## Abilities
**CORE:** **[DEADLY DEMISE D3]**, **[HOVER]**

**Stasis Bomb:** Once per battle, after this model ends a Normal move, you can select one enemy unit (excluding **AIRCRAFT**) it moved over. That unit suffers D3 mortal wounds. Then, roll one D6:
- **1-3:** Until the start of your next Movement phase, that enemy unit cannot Advance or Fall Back.
- **4-6:** Until the start of your next Movement phase, that enemy unit must Remain Stationary.

**Damaged: 1-3 Wounds Remaining:** While this model has 1-3 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
