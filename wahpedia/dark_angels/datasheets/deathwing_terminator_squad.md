# Deathwing Terminator Squad

The backbone of the Deathwing, these veterans are armed with a variety of heavy weapons and are tasked with crushing the enemy's most dangerous units. They are the ultimate expression of the Dark Angels' stubbornness and military might.

## Abilities
**CORE:** **[DEEP STRIKE]**

**Deathwing:**
- Each time a model in this unit makes an attack, you can ignore any or all modifiers to that attack’s Ballistic Skill or Weapon Skill characteristic and/or to the Hit roll.
- Each time a model in this unit makes an attack that targets the enemy unit selected for **Oath of Moment**, add 1 to the Hit roll.

**Watcher in the Dark:** Once per battle, when this unit is targeted by a psychic attack, or when a mortal wound is allocated to this unit, you can use this ability. If you do, until the end of the phase, models in this unit have the **[FEEL NO PAIN 4+]** ability against mortal wounds.

**Teleport Homer:** Once per battle, you can target this unit with the **[RAPID INGRESS]** Stratagem for 0 CP. If you do, you must set this unit up within 3" of your Teleport Homer token and more than 9" horizontally away from all enemy models.
