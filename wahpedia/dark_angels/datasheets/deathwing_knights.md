# Deathwing Knights

The most elite of the Deathwing, these veterans are armed with ancient weapons of the Chapter and are tasked with the most dangerous missions. They are nearly unstoppable in melee and can weather the most intense fire.

## Abilities
**CORE:** **[DEEP STRIKE]**

**Inner Circle:** Each time an attack is allocated to a model in this unit, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack (to a minimum of 1).

**Watcher in the Dark:** Once per battle, when this unit is targeted by a psychic attack, or when a mortal wound is allocated to this unit, you can use this ability. If you do, until the end of the phase, models in this unit have the **[FEEL NO PAIN 4+]** ability against mortal wounds.

**Teleport Homer:** Once per battle, you can target this unit with the **[RAPID INGRESS]** Stratagem for 0 CP. If you do, you must set this unit up within 3" of your Teleport Homer token and more than 9" horizontally away from all enemy models.
