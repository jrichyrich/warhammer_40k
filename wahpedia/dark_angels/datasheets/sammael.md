# Sammael

Grand Master of the Ravenwing, Sammael leads his company from the front, often mounted upon the ancient jetbike Corvex. He is a master of high-speed pursuit and tactical withdrawal.

## Abilities
**CORE:** **[LEADER]**

**Grand Master of the Ravenwing:** While this model is leading a unit, that unit is eligible to shoot and declare a charge in a turn in which it **Advanced**.

**Cut Off Their Escape:** Each time an enemy unit (excluding **MONSTERS** and **VEHICLES**) within Engagement Range of this model's unit is selected to **Fall Back**, models in that enemy unit must take **Desperate Escape** tests as if their unit was Battle-shocked. If they are already Battle-shocked, subtract 1 from those tests.
