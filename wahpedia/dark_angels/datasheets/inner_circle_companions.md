# Inner Circle Companions

Mysterious and silent guardians who have appeared since the return of Lion El'Jonson. They serve as the personal guard for the Chapter's highest leaders, their true identities known only to the Primarch himself.

## Abilities
**CORE:** **[OATH OF MOMENT]**

**Braziers of Judgement:** While a **CHARACTER** model is leading this unit, each time an attack targets this unit, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.

**Enmity for the Unworthy:** Each time a model in this unit makes an attack that targets a **CHARACTER** unit, add 1 to the Hit roll.

**Attached Unit:** If a **CHARACTER** unit from your army with the **Leader** ability can be attached to a **STERNGUARD VETERAN SQUAD**, it can be attached to this unit instead.
