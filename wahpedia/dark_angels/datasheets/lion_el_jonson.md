## Abilities
**CORE:** **[DEEP STRIKE]**, **[FIGHTS FIRST]**

**The Emperor’s Shield:** Each time an attack is allocated to this model, if the Strength of that attack is greater than this model’s Toughness, subtract 1 from the Wound roll. Additionally, if you roll a natural 6 on a successful invulnerable save in melee, the attacker suffers 1 mortal wound.

**Dark Angels Bodyguard:** While this model is within 3" of one or more friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES INFANTRY** units, it has the **[LONE OPERATIVE]** keyword.

**Mist-wreathed Shadow Realms:** In your Command phase, if this unit is not within Engagement Range of any enemy units, you can remove it from the battlefield and place it into Strategic Reserves.

**Watchers in the Dark:** Once per battle, when this model's unit is targeted by a psychic attack, this model has the **[FEEL NO PAIN 4+]** ability against mortal wounds for that phase.

### Primarch of the First Legion (Aura)
At the start of your Command phase, select one of the following auras to last until your next Command phase:
1.  **All Aspects of Tactica:** While a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit is within 6", you can target that unit with a Stratagem even if it has already been targeted with that Stratagem this phase.
2.  **Martial Exemplar:** While a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit is within 6", each time a model in that unit makes a melee attack, you can re-roll the Hit roll.
3.  **No Hiding from the Watchers:** While a friendly **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit is within 6", models in that unit have the **[FEEL NO PAIN 4+]** ability against mortal wounds.
