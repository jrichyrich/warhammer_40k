# Detachment: Company of Hunters

The **Company of Hunters** represents the high-speed pursuit forces of the Dark Angels—the Ravenwing. These warriors are masters of mobile warfare, striking with precision and fading away before the enemy can respond.

## Detachment Rule: Master of Manoeuvre
All **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** units from your army are eligible to shoot in a turn in which they **Advanced** or **Fell Back**.

**BATTLELINE BONUS:** **Outrider Squad** units from your army gain the **BATTLELINE** keyword.

---

## Stratagems

### Hunters’ Trail (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your Command phase.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE** unit from your army that is within range of an objective marker you control.
**EFFECT:** That objective marker remains under your control even if you have no models within range of it, until your opponent controls it at the start or end of any turn.

### Talon Strike (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your Shooting phase or the Fight phase.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE** unit from your army.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time a model in your unit makes an attack that targets an enemy **INFANTRY** or **MOUNTED** unit, add 1 to the Wound roll.

### Death on the Wind (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your Shooting phase, just after an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE** unit from your army has shot.
**TARGET:** One enemy unit that was hit by one or more of those attacks.
**EFFECT:** That enemy unit must take a Battle-shock test.

### High-Speed Focus (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your opponent’s Shooting phase, just after an enemy unit has selected its targets.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE** unit from your army that was selected as a target.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets your unit, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.

### Rapid Reappraisal (1 CP)
**WHEN:** End of your opponent’s Fight phase.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE** unit from your army that is more than 6" away from all enemy units.
**EFFECT:** Place that unit into Strategic Reserves.

### Armor of Contempt (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your opponent’s Shooting phase or the Fight phase, just after an enemy unit has selected its targets.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army that was selected as the target of one or more of the attacking unit’s attacks.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets your unit, worsen the Armour Penetration characteristic of that attack by 1.

## Enhancements

### Master of Manoeuvre (20 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE CHARACTER** model only. The bearer’s unit does not count towards the points limit for Strategic Reserves. Additionally, if the bearer’s unit is in Strategic Reserves, it can arrive in the Reinforcements step of your first, second, or third Movement phase. If it arrives in the first turn, it must be set up within your own deployment zone or no-man's land.

### Recon Hunter (15 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE CHARACTER** model only. The bearer’s unit has the **SCOUTS 9"** ability.

### Mounted Strategist (20 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE CHARACTER** model only. The bearer’s unit is eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which it **Advanced** or **Fell Back**.

### Weapon of the First Legion (10 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED** or **RAVENWING VEHICLE CHARACTER** model only. Add 1 to the Strength and Damage characteristics of the bearer’s melee weapons.
