# Dark Angels: The Unforgiven

The **Dark Angels** are the First Legion of the Space Marines, a brotherhood shrouded in ten millennia of secrets, ritual, and grim determination. They are defined by a hidden shame that has haunted them since the Horus Heresy, a secret war that has shaped their Chapter’s identity for ten thousand years.

## The Secret War and the Fallen
During the Horus Heresy, a portion of the Dark Angels Legion turned traitor on their homeworld of Caliban. These warriors, known as the **Fallen**, were scattered through time and space by a Warp storm as their planet was destroyed. Ever since, the Dark Angels and their Successor Chapters—collectively known as the **Unforgiven**—have obsessively hunted these traitors, seeking to force them to repent or die, thereby washing away the stain on the Legion's honor.

## The Return of the Lion
In the 10th Edition of Warhammer 40,000, the most monumental development for the Chapter is the return of their Primarch, **Lion El'Jonson**. After ten millennia in stasis within the heart of the **Rock** (the Chapter’s asteroid-base), the Lion has awakened. He has returned as a "Knight-Errant" figure, older and wiser, capable of "forest-walking" through the Warp to reach those in need across the Imperium.

### The Risen and the Inner Circle
The Lion’s return has fundamentally shifted the Chapter's mission. He has encountered many of the Fallen who did not truly turn to Chaos but were merely lost or misguided. He has pardoned several of these warriors, reintegrating them as his personal guard, **The Risen**. Mysterious and elite **Inner Circle Companions** now appear from the shadows to guard the Lion and his highest commanders, fighting in absolute silence.

## The Wings of the Lion
The Dark Angels continue to utilize their specialized formations to wage war across the stars:
- **The Deathwing:** The elite 1st Company, consisting of veterans in Terminator armor and the Inner Circle Companions. They are the Chapter’s heavy fist, striking from the heart of the battle.
- **The Ravenwing:** The 2nd Company, masters of high-speed pursuit and reconnaissance. They are the Chapter’s ultimate hunters, tracking the enemies of the Unforgiven across every terrain.

In the era of the **Indomitus Crusade**, the Dark Angels stand as one of the most powerful military forces in the Imperium, led by a living demigod. Yet, they remain haunted by the shadows of their past even as they forge a new and uncertain future.
