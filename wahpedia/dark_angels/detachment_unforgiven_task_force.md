# Detachment: Unforgiven Task Force

The **Unforgiven Task Force** represents the legendary resilience of the Dark Angels, who are known for their "Grim Resolve"—a stubborn refusal to yield even under the most intense psychological and physical pressure.

## Detachment Rule: Grim Resolve
While an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army is Battle-shocked, its Objective Control characteristic is **1** instead of 0.

---

## Stratagems

### Unforgiven Fury (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Shooting phase or Fight phase.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army that has not been selected to shoot or fight this phase.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, weapons equipped by models in your unit have the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability.
**GRIM RESOLVE BONUS:** If any unit in your army is currently Battle-shocked, then until the end of the phase, each time a model in your unit makes an attack, a Critical Hit is scored on a **5+**.

### Armor of Contempt (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your opponent’s Shooting phase or the Fight phase, just after an enemy unit has selected its targets.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army that was selected as the target of one or more of the attacking unit’s attacks.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets your unit, worsen the Armour Penetration characteristic of that attack by 1.

### Intractable (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your Movement phase.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army.
**EFFECT:** Your unit is eligible to shoot and charge in a turn in which it **Fell Back**.

### Fire Discipline (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your Shooting phase.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, ranged weapons equipped by models in your unit have the **[ASSAULT]**, **[HEAVY]**, and **[IGNORES COVER]** abilities.

### Grim Retribution (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your opponent’s Shooting phase, just after an enemy unit has resolved its attacks.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army that had one or more models destroyed by those attacks.
**EFFECT:** Your unit can shoot as if it were your Shooting phase, but it must target the enemy unit that just attacked it, and can only do so if that enemy unit is an eligible target.

### Unbreakable Lines (2 CP)
**WHEN:** Your opponent’s Charge phase, just after an enemy unit has ended a Charge move.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army that is within Engagement Range of that enemy unit.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the turn, each time an attack is allocated to a model in your unit, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack.

## Enhancements

### Shroud of Heroes (25 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES CHARACTER** model only. The first time the bearer is destroyed, roll one D6 at the end of the phase. On a 2+, set the bearer back up on the battlefield as close as possible to where they were destroyed and not within Engagement Range of any enemy units, with 3 wounds remaining.
**GRIM RESOLVE BONUS:** If the bearer was Battle-shocked when they were destroyed, they instead return with their full wounds remaining.

### Stubborn Tenacity (15 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES CHARACTER** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, add 1 to the Hit roll for attacks made by models in that unit if it is below its Starting Strength.
**GRIM RESOLVE BONUS:** If that unit is also Battle-shocked, add 1 to the Wound roll for those attacks as well.

### Heavenfall Blade (20 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES CHARACTER** model only. Add 1 to the Attacks and Strength characteristics of the bearer’s melee weapons.
**GRIM RESOLVE BONUS:** If the bearer is Battle-shocked, add 2 to those characteristics instead.

### Pennant of Remembrance (10 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES ANCIENT** model only. While the bearer is leading a unit, models in that unit have a **6+ Feel No Pain**.
**GRIM RESOLVE BONUS:** If that unit is Battle-shocked, models in that unit have a **4+ Feel No Pain** instead.
