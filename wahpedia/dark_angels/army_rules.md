# Army Rules: Dark Angels

In the 10th Edition of Warhammer 40,000, the Dark Angels share the core army rules of the Adeptus Astartes, but their unique identity is represented through their specialized detachments.

## Oath of Moment
If your Army Faction is **ADEPTUS ASTARTES**, at the start of your Command phase, you can select one unit from your opponent’s army. Until the start of your next Command phase, each time a model from your army with this ability makes an attack that targets that enemy unit, you can re-roll the Hit roll.

## Chapter-Specific Mechanics
While the core army rule is shared across all Space Marine Chapters, the Dark Angels' unique flavor—their stubbornness, the hunt for the Fallen, and their specialized companies—is expressed through their **Detachments**:
- **Unforgiven Task Force:** Focuses on the Chapter's "Grim Resolve," allowing units to hold objectives even when Battle-shocked.
- **Inner Circle Task Force:** Enhances the elite **DEATHWING** and **INFANTRY** units, emphasizing their role as the Chapter's heavy fist.
- **Company of Hunters:** Focuses on the high-speed **RAVENWING** and **MOUNTED** units, granting them superior mobility and firepower.

*Refer to the individual detachment files for detailed rules, stratagems, and enhancements.*
