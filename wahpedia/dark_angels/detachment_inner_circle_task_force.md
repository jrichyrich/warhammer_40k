# Detachment: Inner Circle Task Force

The **Inner Circle Task Force** represents the elite veterans of the Dark Angels—the Deathwing. These warriors are the Chapter’s ultimate hunters and executioners, sworn to secrecy and dedicated to the destruction of the Chapter's most hated foes.

## Detachment Rule: Vowed Target
At the start of your Command phase, select one objective marker on the battlefield. Until the start of your next Command phase, that objective is your **Vowed Objective**.

**EFFECT:** Each time a **DEATHWING INFANTRY** model from your army makes an attack against an enemy unit within range of your Vowed Objective, add 1 to the Wound roll.

---

## Stratagems

### Relic Teleportarium (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your Movement phase.
**TARGET:** One **DEATHWING** unit from your army that is being set up on the battlefield using the Deep Strike ability this phase.
**EFFECT:** Your unit can be set up anywhere on the battlefield that is more than 3" horizontally away from all enemy models.
**RESTRICTION:** A unit set up using this Stratagem is not eligible to declare a charge in the same turn.

### Martyred Retribution (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Any phase, just after an enemy unit has destroyed a **DEATHWING INFANTRY** model from your army.
**TARGET:** That enemy unit.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the battle, each time a **DEATHWING INFANTRY** model from your army makes an attack against that enemy unit, add 1 to the Wound roll.

### Duty Unto Death (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Fight phase, just before an **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army starts to fight.
**TARGET:** One **DEATHWING INFANTRY** unit from your army.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time a model in your unit is destroyed, if that model has not fought, do not remove it from play. The destroyed model can fight after its unit has finished making its attacks, and is then removed from play.
**VOWED OBJECTIVE BONUS:** If your unit is within range of your Vowed Objective, add 1 to the result of the roll to see if that model can fight on death. (Note: This usually refers to the 4+ or 3+ roll).

### Unwavering Duty (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your opponent’s Shooting phase, just after an enemy unit has selected its targets.
**TARGET:** One **DEATHWING INFANTRY** unit from your army that was selected as the target of one or more of the attacking unit’s attacks.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets your unit, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack.

### Armor of Contempt (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Your opponent’s Shooting phase or the Fight phase, just after an enemy unit has selected its targets.
**TARGET:** One **ADEPTUS ASTARTES** unit from your army that was selected as the target of one or more of the attacking unit’s attacks.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets your unit, worsen the Armour Penetration characteristic of that attack by 1.

### Wrath of the Unforgiven (1 CP)
**WHEN:** Fight phase.
**TARGET:** One **DEATHWING INFANTRY** unit from your army.
**EFFECT:** Until the end of the phase, each time a model in your unit makes a melee attack against an enemy unit within range of your Vowed Objective, you can re-roll the Wound roll.

## Enhancements

### Champion of the Deathwing (15 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES CHARACTER** model only. Melee weapons equipped by the bearer have the **[LETHAL HITS]** ability. While the bearer is within range of your Vowed Objective, each time the bearer makes a melee attack, a Critical Hit is scored on a **5+**.

### Deathwing Assault (30 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES CHARACTER** model only. The bearer’s unit can be set up using the Deep Strike ability in the Reinforcements step of your first, second, or third Movement phase, regardless of any mission rules that instruct you otherwise.

### Eye of the Unseen (10 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES CHARACTER** model only. Each time you target the bearer’s unit with a Stratagem, roll one D6. On a 5+, you gain 1 CP. If the bearer is within range of your Vowed Objective, you gain that CP on a 4+ instead.

### Singular Will (20 pts)
**ADEPTUS ASTARTES CHARACTER** model only. Each time the bearer’s unit Piles In or Consolidates, models in that unit can move up to 6" instead of 3".
