# Datasheet: Pallas Grav-attack

The Pallas Grav-attack is a light armored platform that excels in reconnaissance and high-speed harassment. Utilizing advanced repulsor technology, it can navigate the most challenging terrain with ease, while its powerful blaze cannon allows it to engage and destroy a variety of targets. Its role is one of agile and aggressive scouting, often acting as an outrider for larger Custodian formations and ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are identified and eliminated before they can pose a threat.
