# Detachment: Solar Spearhead

This detachment focuses on high-speed armored warfare, utilizing Grav-tanks and Dreadnoughts to spearhead the Emperor's justice.

## Detachment Rules
### **AURIC ARMOUR**
*   While an **ADEPTUS CUSTODES VEHICLE** unit is at Starting Strength, unless it is an **AIRCRAFT** or **Battle-shocked**, add 2 to its Objective Control characteristic.
*   While below Starting Strength, re-roll a Hit roll of 1.
*   While Below Half-strength, re-roll a Hit roll of 1 and a Wound roll of 1.

### **MORITOI ANCIENTS**
*   **ADEPTUS CUSTODES WALKER** units gain +2" Move and +1 to Advance and Charge rolls.
*   **KEYWORDS:** You can select up to 2 **ADEPTUS CUSTODES WALKER** models to gain the **CHARACTER** keyword during muster.

## Enhancements
*   **Adamantine Talisman (25 pts):** Improve Attacks, Strength, and Damage of melee weapons by 1.
*   **Augury Uplink (35 pts):** The bearer has the **Feel No Pain 5+** ability.
*   **Honoured Fallen (Aura) (15 pts):** **VEHICLE** only. Friendly **INFANTRY** or **MOUNTED** within 6" re-roll Hit rolls of 1.
*   **Veteran of the Kataphraktoi (10 pts):** Select a nearby **VEHICLE** or **MOUNTED** unit; it can shoot in a turn in which it Fell Back.

## Stratagems
*   **FLAWLESS CONSTRUCTION (1CP):** (Enemy phase) **VEHICLE** unit subtracts 1 from Wound rolls if S > T.
*   **EMPEROR’S VENGEANCE (1CP):** (Fight phase) Destroyed model can fight before removal on a 4+ (+1 for **WALKERS**).
*   **WRATHFUL ADVANCE (1CP):** (Fight phase) Pile-in moves are D3+3" instead of 3".
*   **UNSTOPPABLE (1CP):** (Movement/Charge phase) **VEHICLE** or **MOUNTED** unit can move through terrain.
*   **RELENTLESS PERSECUTION (1CP):** (Movement phase) **VEHICLE** can shoot after Advancing; **WALKER** can also charge.
*   **PUNISHMENT INESCAPABLE (1CP):** (Shooting phase) Unit ranged weapons gain **[IGNORES COVER]** and ignore BS/Hit modifiers.
