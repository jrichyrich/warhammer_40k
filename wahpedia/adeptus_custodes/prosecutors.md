# Datasheet: Prosecutors

Prosecutors are the foundational infantry of the Sisters of Silence, specialized in identifying and neutralizing enemy psykers. Armed with powerful boltguns and trained in the most advanced anti-psychic tactics, they are able to disrupt the powers of their prey and strike them down with cold, calculated precision. Their role is one of both investigation and execution, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are purged and his servants are shielded from the corruption of the warp.
