# Datasheet: Shield-Captain in Allarus Terminator Armour

Shield-Captains in Allarus Terminator armor are the ultimate heavy shock commanders of the Adeptus Custodes. Clad in the most advanced and durable Terminator suits and wielding devastating guardian spears or castellan axes, they are nearly indestructible anchors on the battlefield. Masters of the teleport strike, they can appear suddenly in the heart of the enemy's lines, their presence providing a significant boost to the tactical and offensive capabilities of their brothers. Their role is one of direct and brutal application of power, ensuring that no enemy can withstand the Emperor's final judgement.

### **Strategic Mastery**
Once per battle round, one unit from your army within 12" of this model can be targeted by a Stratagem for 0CP, even if another unit from your army has already been targeted by that Stratagem this phase.

### **Defiant to the Last**
Each time an attack is allocated to this model, subtract 1 from the Damage characteristic of that attack (to a minimum of 1).
