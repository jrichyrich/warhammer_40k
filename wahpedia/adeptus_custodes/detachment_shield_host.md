# Detachment: Shield Host

This detachment emphasizes the traditional combat mastery and defensive prowess of the Custodian Guard.

## Detachment Rule: Martial Mastery
At the start of the battle round, you can select one of the following abilities. Until the start of the next battle round, that ability applies to all **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** models from your army with the **Martial Ka’tah** ability:
*   Successful unmodified Hit rolls of 5+ score a **Critical Hit** in melee.
*   Improve the **Armour Penetration** characteristic of melee weapons by 1.

## Enhancements
*   **Auric Mantle (15 pts):** **SHIELD-CAPTAIN** or **BLADE CHAMPION** model only. Add 2 to the bearer’s Wounds characteristic.
*   **Castellan’s Mark (20 pts):** **SHIELD-CAPTAIN** model only. Redeploy up to two units after both players have deployed (can be placed into Strategic Reserves).
*   **From the Hall of Armouries (25 pts):** **SHIELD-CAPTAIN** model only. Add 1 to the Strength and Damage characteristics of the bearer’s melee weapons.
*   **Panoptispex (5 pts):** **SHIELD-CAPTAIN** or **BLADE CHAMPION** model only. Ranged weapons in the bearer's unit have **[IGNORES COVER]**.

## Stratagems
*   **ARCANE GENETIC ALCHEMY (1CP):** *Battle Tactic.* (Any phase) Unit gains **4+ Feel No Pain** against mortal wounds.
*   **AVENGE THE FALLEN (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* (Fight phase) Unit below Starting Strength gains +1 Attack (or +2 if below Half-strength).
*   **UNWAVERING SENTINELS (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* (Fight phase) Unit on an objective subtracts 1 from incoming melee Hit rolls.
*   **MULTIPOTENTIALITY (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* (Movement phase) Unit can shoot and charge after Falling Back.
*   **VIGILANCE ETERNAL (1CP):** *Strategic Ploy.* (Movement phase) **BATTLELINE** unit grants Sticky Objectives.
*   **ARCHEOTECH MUNITIONS (1CP):** *Wargear.* (Shooting phase) Unit gains **[LETHAL HITS]** or **[SUSTAINED HITS 1]**.
