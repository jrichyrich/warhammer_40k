# Datasheet: Shield-Captain

Shield-Captains are the frontline commanders of the Adeptus Custodes, veteran warriors whose tactical mastery and individual prowess are unsurpassed. Each one is a hero of legend, having served the Emperor for centuries and proven their worth in countless battles. Clad in the most advanced auramite armor and wielding legendary guardian spears or castellan axes, they lead their brothers from the front, their presence an inspiration to all who fight alongside them. Their role is one of both strategic coordination and direct execution, ensuring that the Emperor's will is carried out with unstoppable force.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Leader**

### **Faction**
*   **Martial Ka’tah**

### **Strategic Mastery**
Once per battle round, one unit from your army within 12" of this model can be targeted by a Stratagem for 0CP, even if another unit from your army has already been targeted by that Stratagem this phase.

### **Golden Light**
Once per battle, at the end of your opponent’s Charge phase, if this model's unit is not within Engagement Range of any enemy units, it can make a Normal move of up to 6".
