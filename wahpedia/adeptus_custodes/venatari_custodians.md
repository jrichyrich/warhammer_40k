# Datasheet: Venatari Custodians

Venatari Custodians are the jump pack-equipped elite of the Adeptus Custodes, specialized in rapid response and airborne assault. Utilizing advanced grav-jump packs, they can leap across the battlefield with supernatural speed, striking from above with their kinetic destroyers or interceptor lances. Their tactical role is one of high-speed disruption and execution, using their mobility to bypass enemy defenses and strike at the heart of their formations. Their presence in a mission is a guarantee of the Emperor's swift and airborne justice.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deep Strike**
*   **Fly**

### **Faction**
*   **Martial Ka’tah**

### **Winged Deliverance**
This unit is eligible to declare a charge in a turn in which it **Fell Back** or **Advanced**.

### **Rapid Deployment**
Once per battle, when this unit is set up on the battlefield using the **Deep Strike** ability, it can be set up anywhere that is more than 3" horizontally away from all enemy units.
