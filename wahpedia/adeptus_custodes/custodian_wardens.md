# Datasheet: Custodian Wardens

Custodian Wardens are veteran warriors who have stood vigil over the Imperial Palace for centuries. They are among the most stoic and resolute of the Ten Thousand, their experience and determination making them nearly immovable on the battlefield. Equipped with legendary guardian spears or castellan axes and protected by ornate auramite armor, they are a formidable presence, able to weather any storm and ensure the protection of vital assets. Their presence in a mission is a symbol of the Emperor's unwavering defense.

## Abilities
### **Core**
*   **Deep Strike**

### **Faction**
*   **Martial Ka’tah**

### **Resolute Will**
While a **CHARACTER** is leading this unit, models in this unit have the **Feel No Pain 4+** ability.

### **Living Fortress**
Once per battle, at the start of any phase, this unit can use this ability. Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets this unit, subtract 1 from the Wound roll.

## Wargear Abilities
### **Vexilla**
Add 1 to the Objective Control characteristic of models in the unit.
