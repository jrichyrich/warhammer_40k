# Datasheet: Anathema Psykana Rhino

The Anathema Psykana Rhino is the standard armored transport of the Sisters of Silence, designed to deliver squads of these specialized warriors safely into the heart of the enemy's lines. Its rugged construction and ease of maintenance make it an essential asset for any mission involving the identification and neutralization of psykers. Equipped with self-repair systems and a versatile firing deck, it provides a mobile base of operations while protecting its passengers from the corrupting influence of the warp. Its role is one of armored delivery and tactical support, ensuring that the Emperor's enemies are identifiable only by the silence they leave behind.
