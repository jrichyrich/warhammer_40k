# Detachment: Auric Champions

This detachment focuses on the heroic deeds and leadership of individual Custodian champions.

## Detachment Rule: Assemblage of Might
At the start of your Command phase, select one unit from your opponent’s army. Until the start of your next Command phase, each time a model in an **ADEPTUS CUSTODES CHARACTER** unit from your army makes an attack that targets that enemy unit, add 1 to the Wound roll.

## Enhancements
*   **Blade Imperator (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. On charge, deal D3 mortal wounds on a 4+. Once per battle, force Battle-shock within 6".
*   **Inspirational Exemplar (10 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. 5+ Leadership. Once per battle, remove Battle-shock from a unit within 12".
*   **Martial Philosopher (30 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. Shoot and/or charge after Falling Back. Once per battle, 6" Normal move when enemy ends move within 9".
*   **Veiled Blade (25 pts):** **ADEPTUS CUSTODES** model only. +2 Attacks. Once per battle, triple OC for one turn.

## Stratagems
*   **SLAYER OF CHAMPIONS (1CP):** *Epic Deed.* (Any phase) **CHARACTER** unit that destroys the selected target marks a new enemy unit for +1 to Wound.
*   **SUPERHUMAN RESERVES (2CP):** *Epic Deed.* (Any phase) Warlord can use a "Once per battle" ability a second time.
*   **THE EMPEROR’S AUSPICE (1CP):** *Epic Deed.* (Enemy phase) **CHARACTER** models in a unit gain **4+ Feel No Pain**.
*   **EARNING OF A NAME (1CP):** *Epic Deed.* (Fight phase) Two **CHARACTER** units re-roll Hit/Wound rolls against **MONSTERS** or **VEHICLES**.
*   **VIGIL UNENDING (2CP):** *Epic Deed.* (Fight phase) Destroyed **CHARACTER** fights before removal.
*   **SHOULDER THE MANTLE (1CP):** *Epic Deed.* (Movement phase) Lone **CHARACTER** attaches to a nearby friendly unit.
