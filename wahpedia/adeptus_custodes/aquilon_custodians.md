# Datasheet: Aquilon Custodians

Aquilon Custodians are elite heavy shock infantry of the Adeptus Custodes, clad in massive suits of Aquilon Terminator armor and armed with devastating power gauntlets and specialized ranged weaponry. They are masters of the teleport strike, appearing suddenly in the heart of the enemy's lines to unleash a whirlwind of destruction. Their tactical role is one of decapitation and disruption, using their extreme durability and overwhelming firepower to eliminate high-value targets and scatter enemy formations. Their presence in a mission is a guarantee of the Emperor's final judgement.
