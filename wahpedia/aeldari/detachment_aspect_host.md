# Detachment: Aspect Host

## Detachment Rule: Path of the Warrior
*   Select one for unit when shooting/fighting: **Re-roll Hit rolls of 1** OR **Re-roll Wound rolls of 1**.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warrior Focus** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Ignore all modifiers to attacks. |
| **To Their Final Breath** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Fight on death on 4+ (bonus if Aspect Shrine token removed). |
| **Skyborne Sanctuary** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Embark in transport at end of Fight phase. |
| **Doom Inescapable** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | **AVATAR OF KHAINE:** Wailing Doom becomes 18" Range, Damage 8. |
| **Preternatural Precision** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Gain **[Ignores Cover], [Lethal Hits]**, or **[Sustained Hits 1]**. |
| **Khaine’s Vengeance** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Force Desperate Escape tests on units Falling Back. |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Aspect of Murder (25 pts):** *AUTARCH only.* +1 Damage and **[Precision]** in melee.
*   **Mantle of Wisdom (30 pts):** *AUTARCH only.* Leading unit gains both Path of the Warrior abilities.
*   **Shimmerstone (15 pts):** *AUTARCH only.* -1 to Wound roll against leading unit.
*   **Strategic Savant (15 pts):** *AUTARCH only.* +1 OC to models in leading unit.
