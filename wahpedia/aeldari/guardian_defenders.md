### Abilities
**Faction:** Strands of Fate

**Fleet of Foot**
This unit can perform the Fade Back Agile Manoeuvre without spending a Battle Focus token.

**Crewed Platform**
When the last Guardian Defender is destroyed, any remaining Heavy Weapon Platforms are also destroyed.

### Unit Composition
*   **10 Guardian Defenders**
*   **1 Heavy Weapon Platform**

**Equipped with:**
*   **Guardian Defender:** Shuriken catapult, close combat weapon.
*   **Heavy Weapon Platform:** Shuriken cannon, close combat weapon.

**Wargear Options:**
The Heavy Weapon Platform's shuriken cannon can be replaced with one of the following:
* 1 bright lance
* 1 Aeldari missile launcher
* 1 scatter laser
* 1 starcannon
