# Detachment: Windrider Host

## Detachment Rule: Ride the Wind
*   **ASURYANI MOUNTED/VYPER** units can be set up from Reserves as if the battle round were one higher.
*   At the end of your opponent's turn, place 1-3 **MOUNTED/VYPER** units into Strategic Reserves.
*   **WINDRIDERS** gain the **BATTLELINE** keyword.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Death From On High** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Re-roll Wound rolls for unit arriving from Reserves this turn. |
| **Overflight** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Unit can make a 7" Normal move after destroying an enemy unit. |
| **Wind of Blades** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Unit is eligible to shoot and charge after Advancing or Falling Back. |
| **Daring Riders** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Set up from Reserves more than 6" away (cannot charge if within 9"). |
| **Focused Firepower** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Improve AP characteristic of attacks by 1. |
| **Spiralling Evasion** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Unit gains a 4+ invulnerable save. |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Firstdrawn Blade (10 pts):** *ASURYANI MOUNTED only.* Unit gains **Scouts 9"**.
*   **Mirage Field (25 pts):** *ASURYANI MOUNTED only.* -1 to Hit rolls against unit.
*   **Seersight Strike (15 pts):** *ASURYANI MOUNTED PSYKER only.* Psychic weapons gain **[Anti-Monster 2+]** and **[Anti-Vehicle 2+]**.
*   **Echoes of Ulthanesh (20 pts):** *ASURYANI MOUNTED only.* On a 5+ (with bonuses for location), gain 1CP.
