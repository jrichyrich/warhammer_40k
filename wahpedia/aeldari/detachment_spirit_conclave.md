# Detachment: Spirit Conclave

## Detachment Rule: Shepherds of the Dead
*   When a **PSYKER** is destroyed, the killer gets a Vengeful Dead token. **WRAITH CONSTRUCTS** get +1 to Hit/Wound against units with tokens.
*   **Spirit Guides (Aura):** **WRAITH** units within 12" of a **PSYKER** gain Battle Focus.
*   **WRAITHBLADES** and **WRAITHGUARD** gain **BATTLELINE**.

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Seer’s Eye** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Wraith unit ignores modifiers to AP and Damage against a specific target. |
| **Wraithbone Armour** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | -1 Damage to attacks against Wraith unit (excluding Titanic). |
| **Blades from Beyond** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Melee weapons gain **[Devastating Wounds]**. |
| **Soul Bridge** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Wraith unit is considered within 12" of a PSYKER for abilities. |
| **Spirit Token** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Objective markers controlled by Wraith units remain under your control even if you leave them. |
| **Crushing Strides** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Mortal wounds on the charge (D6 per model based on keywords). |

## Enhancements
*   **Light of Clarity (30 pts):** *SPIRITSEER only.* +1 OC to Wraith INFANTRY, +3 OC to Wraith MONSTERS within 12".
*   **Stave of Kurnous (15 pts):** *SPIRITSEER only.* Wraith unit within 12" gains **[Precision]** on Critical Wounds.
*   **Rune of Mists (10 pts):** *SPIRITSEER only.* Wraith unit within 12" gains Benefit of Cover if attacker is >18" away.
*   **Higher Duty (25 pts):** *SPIRITSEER only.* Unit can make 6" Normal move if enemy ends move within 9".
