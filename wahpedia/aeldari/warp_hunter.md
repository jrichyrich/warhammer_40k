# Datasheet: Warp Hunter

The Warp Hunter is a specialized tank that utilizes a D-flail to tear holes in reality, unleashing the devastating energies of the warp upon its foes. Armed with this terrifying weapon, it can engage a variety of targets, while its high speed and maneuverability allow it to redeploy quickly to where its reality-warping power is needed most. Its role is one of specialized fire support, often acting as a disruptor for enemy lines and a harbinger of doom for those who oppose the Aeldari.

*   **Strands of Fate**

### **Devastating Assault**
In your Shooting phase, after this model has shot, select one enemy unit hit by one or more attacks made by this model this phase. That enemy unit must take a Battle-shock test.

### **Damaged: 1-4 Wounds Remaining**
While this model has 1-4 wounds remaining, each time it makes an attack, subtract 1 from the Hit roll.
