# Detachment: Seer Council

## Detachment Rule: Strands of Fate
*   Generate 3/6/9 Fate dice at start of battle.
*   Discard specific Fate dice values to reduce Stratagem costs by 1CP.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Presentiment of Dread** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Force Battle-shock test at -1 within 18". |
| **Forewarned** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | -1 to Hit and Wound against unit within 9" of PSYKER. |
| **Unshrouded Truth** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Reposition unit within 9" of PSYKER anywhere >9" from enemy. |
| **Fate Inescapable** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | **[Ignores Cover]** and improved AP on Critical Wounds. |
| **Isha’s Fury** | 1CP | Epic Deed | Mortal wounds to enemy ending move within 9" of PSYKER. |
| **Psychic Shield** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Unit can only be targeted within 18". |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Lucid Eye (30 pts):** *ASURYANI PSYKER only.* +/- 1 to a Fate dice value in Command phase.
*   **Runes of Warding (25 pts):** *ASURYANI PSYKER only.* Unit gains FNP 4+ against Mortal/Psychic/Devastating.
*   **Stone of Eldritch Fury (15 pts):** *ASURYANI PSYKER only.* +12" Range to ranged Psychic weapons.
*   **Torc of Morai-Heg (20 pts):** *ASURYANI PSYKER only.* Increase opponent's Stratagem cost by 1CP.
