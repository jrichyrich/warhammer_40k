# Detachment: Guardian Battlehost

## Detachment Rule: Defend at All Costs
*   **DIRE AVENGER, GUARDIAN, SUPPORT WEAPON**, or **WAR WALKER** units get +1 to Hit if they or the target are within range of an objective marker.

---

## Stratagems
| Stratagem | Cost | Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warding Salvoes** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Re-roll Wound rolls against targets on objectives. |
| **Shield Nodes** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | -1 to Wound roll against unit if within range of an objective. |
| **Vaul’s Vengeance** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | WAR WALKER unit can shoot back after a Guardian/Avenger unit is destroyed. |
| **Time to Strike** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | STORM GUARDIANS auto-advance 6" and can shoot/charge. |
| **Blades of Asuryan** | 1CP | Battle Tactic | Ranged weapons gain **[Pistol]**. |
| **Cost of Victory** | 1CP | Strategic Ploy | Remove unit to Strategic Reserves and return all destroyed models. |

---

## Enhancements
*   **Craftworld’s Champion (25 pts):** *ASURYANI only.* Bearer has OC 5.
*   **Ethereal Pathway (30 pts):** *ASURYANI only.* Up to two **GUARDIAN** units gain **Infiltrators**.
*   **Protector of the Paths (20 pts):** *ASURYANI only.* Leading unit can use Overwatch for 0CP; hits on 5+ (4+ on objective).
*   **Breath of Vaul (10 pts):** *ASURYANI only.* Leading **STORM GUARDIANS** re-roll flamer attack counts and fusion damage.
