# Aeldari Crusade Rules

## Guiding Fate
**Dire Premonitions:** Prevent Threat from reaching 13. Generate 3 Threads of Fate (Destined Device, Rising Nemesis, etc.).

**Growing Threats:** Add D3 Threat at end of battle (3 if lost).

**Changing the Future:** Use Fulcrum of Fate Agenda to end Threads.

**Thread Stages:** Dawning (Reduce Threat by 3), Waning (2), Frayed (1).

## Destined Device Thread
*   **Buried Provenance:** Control specific objective to find device.
*   **Unknowing Bearer:** Destroy specific enemy unit carrying device.
